## CRAFT NOTES

This is the longest "ordinary day" the player gets in the entire campaign. It will feel ordinary because *it is*. The narrator should resist the temptation to thicken the foreshadowing — one phone lag, one TV banner, one masked bookseller is enough. Sunday is the day the texture of the *before* lands hardest because it is the last full day of it.

The phone networks lagging is the first concrete failure of the world's infrastructure the player sees. Don't over-explain. Maya's "ugh" when the call doesn't connect is enough.
