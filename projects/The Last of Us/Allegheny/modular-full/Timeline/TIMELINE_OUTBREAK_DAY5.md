### Day 5 — Wednesday

**AWARENESS tier: 3**

**The first defended day.** People are in their homes or barricaded in groups. Whoever is alive on Day 5 is mostly going to be alive on Day 6.

**Offscreen events:**

- FEDRA establishes the first perimeter walls around what will become the downtown QZ. Plywood and chain-link initially.
- The Convention Center downtown is now a FEDRA distribution and triage point.
- The first FEDRA-issued **ration cards** are being printed.
- Pittsburgh's power grid drops by half during the afternoon; many neighborhoods are dark by sundown.

**On the player's channels:**

- TV is now intermittent. Local stations still operate.
- Phones are largely dead for calls; texts are slow but possible.
- Radio: EAS broadcasts continue. NPR has gone to a satellite-fed national broadcast.
- The first **FEDRA leaflets** are dropped or distributed: "Quarantine zones are being established. Cooperate with personnel for your safety and the safety of others."

**NPCs:**

- **Erin** has been operating out of the Thunderbird as an informal aid station. She has been triaging her neighbors. The bar is now functionally closed but Erin sleeps on the floor of the back room.
- **Victor** is fully active. He has begun supplying Maya's group from his duffel.
- **Ray** is now on continuous duty. Her civilian paramedic job has been merged with the National Guard medical response. She is wearing both her civilian uniform and a green armband.
