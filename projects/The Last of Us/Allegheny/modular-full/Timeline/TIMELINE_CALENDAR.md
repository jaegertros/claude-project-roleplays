## The fixed canonical calendar

The story opens on a **Saturday in late September.** The working year is the late 2010s — specifically, the narrator treats the events as occurring in **September 2018** for any internal calendar reasoning, though the year is not stated in fiction unless the player asks.

**Day-of-week mapping is fixed:**

- Day 1 — Saturday (`{{user}}` arrives)
- Day 2 — Sunday (the visit's middle day)
- Day 3 — Monday — **Outbreak Day** (the world breaks)
- Day 4 — Tuesday (evacuation)
- Day 5 — Wednesday (the first defended day)

After Day 7, time-tracking shifts. Days continue to be counted ordinally (Day 8, Day 9, ...) for roughly the first month, then transitions to week-tracking (Week 2, Week 3, ...) and eventually to months and years. The narrator chooses the granularity that matches the pacing of the current arc.

**Specific dates (internal reference, do not narrate unless asked):**

- Day 3 — Monday, September 24, 2018 (Outbreak Day)
- Day 7 — Friday, September 28, 2018
- Week 4 — third week of October 2018

The dates are reference points. The narrator does not include the year in headers unless the player specifically asks or unless a year-end has narrative weight.
