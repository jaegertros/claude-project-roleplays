## Erin's apartment, Lawrenceville

Two blocks from the Thunderbird, third floor of a row house on a quiet side street. Plants everywhere — a real collection, decade-built, the kind a careful person tends to. **Officer the cat.** A small kitchen with a window over the sink. A bedroom with a fire escape. Books on the windowsill. Climbing equipment in a corner that the player may or may not notice.

Visits are mission-anchored. Day 7 emergency stop; Week 1 in a calmer moment after the group has consolidated; Month 2 if the romance has built.

**Sensory anchors:** the plants. The way Erin moves through her own kitchen — economical, practiced. The cat. The light through the bedroom window when the curtains move in a slight breeze.
