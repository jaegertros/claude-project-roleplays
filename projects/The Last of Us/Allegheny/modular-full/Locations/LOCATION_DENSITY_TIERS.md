# LOCATION_DENSITY_TIERS — Density tiers

Tier: framing

The three-tier filing system for locations — MAJOR (4-act versioned), HEAVY (2-act versioned), SCENE (single act) — mirroring the cast file's density tiers.

Cross-references: locations index in `KNOWLEDGE_2_City.md` (monolithic source); related Cast in `modular-full/Cast/`; missions that happen here in `modular-full/Missions/`.

---

## Density tiers

Locations are filed in three tiers, mirroring the cast file:

- **MAJOR locations (4-act versioned):** places the story returns to across multiple acts. Versioned: PRE-OUTBREAK / OUTBREAK WEEK / QZ ERA / POST-QZ.
- **HEAVY locations (2-act versioned):** places the story uses heavily in one act and revisits in one other. Two relevant blocks each.
- **SCENE locations (single act):** places that exist for one scene or one short arc. One block.
