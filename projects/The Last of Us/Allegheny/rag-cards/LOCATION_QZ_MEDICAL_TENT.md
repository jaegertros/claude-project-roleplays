## The QZ medical tent (Convention Center / Week 2+)

A converted hall in the Convention Center, downtown. Cots in rows. Triage signage. Civilian volunteers in scrubs. **Erin** ends up here in Week 1, pulled by competence into the EMT work she swore off. **Martha** (Erin's ex-partner) is here. The work is medicine practiced under field conditions — limited supplies, no continuity of care, infection routes everyone is trying not to think about.

**Sensory anchors:** the high ceiling of the Convention Center. The fluorescent lights, some out, some flickering. The institutional smell of an unventilated space full of bodies. The sound of suffering being managed by people who are good at managing it.
