### Week 4 (Days 22–28)

**AWARENESS tier: 4**

**Late October.** Frost in the mornings. The first deaths in the QZ from causes other than infection — pneumonia, untreated chronic conditions, despair.

The mission M-13 (The Smuggler's Job) anchors here or in Week 5. The group is offered a paying job that requires crossing the wall. This is the first major moral question of the new world for them.

Maya's mother has not been contacted in twenty-five days. Maya knows.
