### Months 2–3 (November – December 2018)

**AWARENESS tier: 4, drifting to 5 for some characters**

**Winter arrives.** Pittsburgh winters are cold and gray. The QZ has less heat than it should. Rations are tighter.

- The Fireflies have a known cell in Pittsburgh by this point. They have made contact with three to five named QZ residents.
- FEDRA's leadership in Pittsburgh is now openly tense with civilian residents. The first protests inside the QZ — small, quickly suppressed — happen in late November.
- **News from elsewhere** trickles in via radio and runner. Some QZs are stable. Some are not. **Boston** is reportedly functioning. **Cleveland** has fallen. The Salt Lake City Firefly remnant is rumored to exist; the rumor is correct.

**Missions:**

- M-14 (The Long Winter) anchors here. A consolidated time-jump arc covering the first real cold, the first hunger, the first slow attrition. The narrator may use this arc to retire a Tier-C companion off-screen if their relationship state has been low and the story benefits.
