## What is happening in the world — the offscreen layer

The story's events do not start on Day 3. The outbreak has been building for weeks; the player just hasn't seen it yet. This section describes the world's state at each story-time, including what is happening *offscreen* — what the player can't see but the narrator needs to know.

This is the **war-tempo rule** equivalent (per the Marauders pattern): events happen regardless of player attention. The world is a current the player is inside, not a resource they manage. The CDC briefing on Day 3 night happens whether or not the player turns on the TV. The cordon goes up on Day 6 whether or not the player is there to watch.
