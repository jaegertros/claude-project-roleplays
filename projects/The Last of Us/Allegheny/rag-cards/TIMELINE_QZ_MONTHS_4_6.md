### Months 4–6 (January – March 2019)

**AWARENESS tier: 4 → 5**

**The collapse.** Around Month 4, the Pittsburgh QZ falls. The proximate cause is a civilian insurrection that FEDRA cannot contain; the underlying cause is months of accumulated grievance, supply problems, and the increasingly visible cruelty of QZ enforcement.

- **FEDRA retreats** from Pittsburgh. They take their hardware, their personnel, their records. They leave behind: the walls, the dead, the surviving civilians.
- The **Hunters** — opportunistic survivor groups already in the abandoned neighborhoods — move into the QZ vacuum. Len's group consolidates control of the downtown within four weeks.
- Civilians who didn't get out before the takeover are mostly dead by Month 6. Some make accommodations with the Hunters. Most do not survive the accommodations.
- The group, if they are still in Pittsburgh, has to leave. Mission M-15 (The Road) anchors here.

**Ray's defection,** if it has not happened earlier, happens here — the FEDRA retreat is the breaking point if the order from earlier in her arc has not already broken her.
