### Day 7 — Friday

**AWARENESS tier: 3, climbing to 4 for some characters**

**The leaving day.** Whoever is going to leave Pittsburgh is leaving today or tomorrow. The QZ is now hard-bordered for those inside. Those outside the wall must decide.

**Offscreen events:**

- Pittsburgh's residential neighborhoods outside the QZ are being abandoned in waves. Some residents move to the QZ for the rations and processed protection. Some leave the city entirely. Some barricade in place.
- FEDRA's policy hardens: civilians who refuse to enter the QZ are *not* offered evacuation. They are "encouraged" to enter or to leave the FEDRA-controlled territory.
- The first major outbreak event *inside* the early QZ: a missed bite-detection on Day 6 turns a civilian inside the perimeter. FEDRA's response is fast and ugly. Word travels fast within the QZ.

**On the player's channels:**

- TV: gone for most.
- Phones: gone for most. Some local mesh-network communications are starting to surface — improvised radio, runners between buildings.
- Radio: FEDRA emergency broadcasts only.

**NPCs:**

- **Sweeps** of residential buildings begin in some neighborhoods. M-10 ("The Sweep") in the mission deck is anchored to this day or the next, depending on the player's location.
- Maya's apartment is left today — either by force, by choice, or by the building's evacuation.
