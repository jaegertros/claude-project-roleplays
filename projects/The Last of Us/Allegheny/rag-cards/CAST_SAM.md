# CAST_SAM — Samuel "Sam" Mendez

Tier: non_romance_male · character_id: SAM · picked up Week 2 or 3 in the QZ or near it (most commonly McKees Rocks)

Sam is sixteen, from McKees Rocks, separated from his older sister Marisol on Day 3. He is the campaign's young presence — not a child, not a peer, but a complication the group accepts when they pick him up. This file contains his MID-tier sheet.

Cross-references: his pickup is improvisational within Week 2-3, often anchored to an encounter outside the QZ; his sister-search arc resolves in late Year 1 or never; his depth beat (telling the group about Marisol) is improvisational, often in Month 3.

## SAM — the teenage pickup

### identity

- **name**: Samuel "Sam" Mendez
- **age**: 16
- **pronouns**: he/him
- **tier**: non_romance_male
- **character_id**: SAM
- **role_in_world**: A kid the group picks up in Week 2 or 3. Not a child; not a peer. The complication of having a young person in the group.
- **occupation_or_program**: Tenth grade, two months ago. Cross-country runner.
- **hometown**: Pittsburgh, McKees Rocks specifically.
- **living_situation**: Picked up alone. Family situation will surface slowly — see hidden.
- **joins**: Week 2 or 3, depending on group's location.
- **mortality**: mortal — earned. *He is sixteen; the narrator should be careful about how he dies if he dies. The casualty list has him low.*

### the engine

- **core_want**: To find his older sister, who he was separated from on Day 3. He does not know if she is alive. He does not know how to find her. He cannot stop looking.
- **core_fear**: That he is already on his own.

### voice

- **speech_summary**: Quick, sharp, sometimes funny without meaning to be. Teenager voice — but not the cartoon version.
- **rhythm**: Fast. Interrupts. Trails off.
- **three_speech_tics**:
  - "Whatever" used in a non-dismissive way — more like "okay, moving on"
  - Says "for real" as a punctuation mark
  - Will not call anyone "sir" or "ma'am" no matter how Pittsburgh that is
- **two_dialogue_examples**:
  - *(Week 2, AWARENESS 4, first encounter, behind a dumpster in McKees Rocks, holding a bent piece of rebar and visibly trying not to look scared)* "I'm not — I don't want any — whatever, I'm just here. I'm not, like. Are you with anybody? Like is there a group. For real. I'm asking for real."
  - *(Month 3, AWARENESS 5, sitting on the back of a truck eating a can of something cold, after Maya has asked about his sister)* "She's older. By like three years. Marisol. She — yeah. Marisol. She's got a tattoo on her wrist, it's like a — yeah. Whatever. I'm gonna find her. I'm gonna. For real."

### trait_expansions

- **trait: scared**
  - surface_behavior: Quick to move, quick to talk, quick to act. The motion is the cover.
  - underlying_reason: Sixteen, alone, on Day 7 he watched something he won't talk about.
  - limit: He has not let himself stop, because if he stops he will fall apart.
  - scene_expression: Sam runs fast. He volunteers for the things no one volunteers for. The narrator should not over-pathologize — he is also a competent kid doing his best.

- **trait: looking**
  - surface_behavior: Watches people's faces. Reads strangers from twenty feet away. Looks for one specific person.
  - underlying_reason: His sister.
  - limit: The looking is the thing keeping him going.
  - scene_expression: When the group passes a window, Sam looks in. When they pass a runner, Sam looks. The narrator should render this without commentary.

### states

- **default_state**: Watchful, quick, slightly performed.
- **under_stress**: Talks more.
- **when_hurt**: Doesn't tell anyone. Patches it himself. Will limp.
