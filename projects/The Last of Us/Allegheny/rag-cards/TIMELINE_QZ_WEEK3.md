### Week 3 (Days 15–21)

**AWARENESS tier: 4**

**The Firefly pitch lands.** Casey makes their first overture to a member of the group (most commonly Ray, but the narrator selects based on relationship state). The mission M-12 (The Pamphlet) is anchored here.

The QZ's bureaucracy is now visible as a system, not as an emergency response. The cruelty is procedural. FEDRA's officers are not (mostly) sadistic — they are following orders that have, in three weeks, hardened from "public health enforcement" into "population management."

**The word "Cordyceps"** is now in QZ-civilian vocabulary. People say it without flinching. It has been three weeks; the word is no longer new.

**The word "Fireflies"** enters QZ vocabulary this week if the player has had a Firefly contact. Otherwise it remains absent.
