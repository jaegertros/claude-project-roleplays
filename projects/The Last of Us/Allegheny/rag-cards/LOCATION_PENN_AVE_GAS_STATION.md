## A gas station and convenience store on Penn Avenue, near East Liberty

Where **Tina** works on Day 4. A typical Pittsburgh gas station with a convenience store — Pennsylvania Lottery scratchers, beef jerky, motor oil, a pizza counter that nobody buys from after 10 PM. By the time the group passes through on Day 4, the place is running on emergency power, the pumps are out, but Tina is still behind the counter. By the time they pass back on Day 6, the place is dark.

**Sensory anchors:** fluorescent light on linoleum; the smell of old pizza; the dispenser racks of car-care products; the bell on the door that still works.
