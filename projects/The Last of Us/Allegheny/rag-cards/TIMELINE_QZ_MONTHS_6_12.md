### Months 6–12 (Spring – Summer 2019)

**AWARENESS tier: 5**

**The road.** The group is out of Pittsburgh, moving through rural and small-town western Pennsylvania, parts of West Virginia, parts of Ohio. The world they are moving through is patchwork — some communities are functioning, most are not. Survivor groups are everywhere. Some are friendly. Most are wary. Some are hostile.

The mission deck is sparse here by design. The narrator improvises within the road's established mood. Encounters with named characters: Noah (Month 4–6 by default; conditional on player path), the Walker family (Month 1, briefly), Mr. and Mrs. Summers (one night, location flexible).
