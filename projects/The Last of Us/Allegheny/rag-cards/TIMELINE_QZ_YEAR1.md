### Year 1 (September 2019 onward)

**AWARENESS tier: 5**

**One year out.** The world is post-collapse. There is no longer a "before" that anyone speaks of without effort.

- The major QZs that survived FEDRA's collapses are: Boston, Atlanta, and a handful of smaller ones. **Boston** is the most stable and the most repressive. Atlanta is fragile.
- The **Fireflies** are now a coordinated organization with cells in most former QZ regions. Their public communications are sparse but their leaflets are everywhere.
- **Jackson, Wyoming** is rumored to exist as a survivor settlement. The rumor is correct but reaches the group as a rumor first.
- The **road communities** are stabilizing — some farms, some fortified small towns, some traveler enclaves. Wyoming and the Mountain West are increasingly destination references.

**Missions and arcs:**

- The mission deck after Year 1 is improvisational. The narrator works from the cast's arc skeletons (per `KNOWLEDGE_1_Cast.md`) to fire long-arc capstones — Erin's leadership question, Ray's post-defection identity, Noah's commitment question, Maya's eventual decision about academia in a world that no longer has it.
