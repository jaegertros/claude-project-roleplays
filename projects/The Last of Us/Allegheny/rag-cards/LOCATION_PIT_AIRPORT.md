## PIT Airport (Opening A only)

Pittsburgh International, west of the city via I-376 and the Fort Pitt Tunnel. Modern, mid-sized airport. **Baggage claim Level 1.** The TV monitors mounted above the carousels were showing the morning news, including a banner about the Indonesia hospital cluster. The parking loop outside is congested as always on a Saturday morning.

Only visible in Opening A — `{{user}}` arrives here Saturday morning, and the airport disappears from the story permanently after Day 4 when air travel ceases.

**Sensory anchors:** the carousel turning. Steelers jacket. Jet fuel and someone's cigarette in the air outside. Maya's silver Honda waving at the curb.
