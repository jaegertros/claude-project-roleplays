### Day 6 — Thursday

**AWARENESS tier: 3**

**The cordon day.** FEDRA establishes its first hard perimeter. Civilians who are inside the perimeter are processed for QZ entry; civilians outside are increasingly on their own.

**Offscreen events:**

- 8 AM: FEDRA announces the **Pittsburgh Quarantine Zone (QZ)** is being formally established. Boundaries are: downtown, parts of the South Side, parts of Mount Washington. Civilians are directed to specific entry points for processing.
- 10 AM: First processed civilians inside the QZ.
- Throughout the day: bite-detection scanning at all entry points. Failed scans result in "isolation" (a euphemism the player will eventually understand).
- 4 PM: First confirmed FEDRA shooting of a bite-positive civilian. The shooting is procedurally correct per the orders FEDRA has been issued. It is not the last.

**On the player's channels:**

- TV: largely off. Most local stations have ceased broadcasting.
- Phones: dead for most uses. Some text-message networks intermittently functional.
- Radio: FEDRA-controlled emergency broadcasts on emergency frequencies. NPR national still broadcasting from somewhere.
- **FEDRA loudspeaker trucks** are now driving through Pittsburgh neighborhoods broadcasting instructions. They say: "Proceed to the nearest checkpoint for processing. Do not approach checkpoints with weapons. Do not approach if you have been bitten or scratched."

**NPCs:**

- **Drake** (FEDRA lieutenant) is now in command of QZ Sector 4 cordon (whichever sector the player's group passes through). He has been on duty for fifty-two hours and is performing procedure.
- **Ray** has been transferred from civilian medical to FEDRA enlisted under emergency federalization. She is in uniform now.
- **Mike's status** depends on the run — if he has survived to Day 6, he is with the group. If he has been lost, the narrator selected the moment per `KNOWLEDGE_6_Missions.md`.
