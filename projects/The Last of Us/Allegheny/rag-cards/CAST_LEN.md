# CAST_LEN — Lenny "Len" Pasternak

Tier: antagonist · character_id: LEN · first contact Month 4+; encounter optional based on player route

Len is a lieutenant in the Hunter faction that takes Pittsburgh after FEDRA's collapse. His character is friendliness as menace — frightening because of the friendliness, not in spite of it. This file contains his MID-tier sheet.

Cross-references: his appearances are improvisational within the road era; his Pittsburgh territory makes him a threat if the group ever returns to the city after Month 4; he is the face of the Hunter-takeover era.
