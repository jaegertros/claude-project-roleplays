# RP04-SC00 — McGonagall enters the hidden chamber and finds the black pool

**In-story date:** Sunday 11 September 1977, 09:24  
**Location:** Hidden room off the prefect corridor / behind the sconce-seam  
**Present characters:** McGonagall, Caleb, Aisling, Brigid  
**Status:** Canon; continues `RP03-SC33`

## What happened
McGonagall crossed the threshold first with Lumos. The hidden chamber proved larger than the corridor footprint should allow: bare cold stone, no far wall in reach, and a still black pool set flush into the floor. The pool held light rather than reflecting it. At its lip were two worn kneeling-hollows, side by side, with undisturbed grey dust over both. McGonagall ordered Caleb and Aisling to hold the door and forbade anyone from approaching the water.

## Important canon established
- The hidden room is not a normal worked castle room; it is a deep, cold, stone chamber larger than expected.
- The black pool does not reflect light; it holds/absorbs it in a night-water-like way.
- There are exactly two kneeling hollows at the pool's lip, a hand's width apart.
- No one has knelt there for a long time; dust is unbroken.
- McGonagall immediately reads the room as dangerous enough to control access.

## Character / relationship beats
- McGonagall takes command without theatrical alarm.
- Caleb and Aisling are trusted to hold the door, not investigate freely.

## Objects / materials / mechanics introduced
- Hidden black pool / natural-well chamber.
- Two kneeling hollows.
- Door-holding protocol for the threshold.

## Quotes worth preserving
> “Hold the door. Nobody comes near the water.”

## Open hooks
- What is the black water?
- Why are there two kneeling places?
- What relation does the room have to the shrine and Merphila?

# RP04-SC18 — Salve hair tests reveal a bond-sensitive binding behavior

**In-story date:** Sunday 11 September 1977, 14:33–14:40  
**Location:** Brigham's office / Bone Zone  
**Present characters:** Caleb, Mei, Lin  
**Status:** Canon

## What happened

Additional Caleb hairs sank but did not trigger the same response. Mei's hair triggered a fresh warmth/binding response. Lin's hair sank but did nothing.
