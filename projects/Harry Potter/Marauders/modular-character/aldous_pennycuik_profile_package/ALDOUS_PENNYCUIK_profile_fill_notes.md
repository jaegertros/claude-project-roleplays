## Main correction

Pennycuik is not just “the mentor” and not just “the unicorn doctor.”

He is both:

1. **Imogen’s professional future made personal** — the person whose post she is trying to inherit.
2. **The active field Healer** at the White Lightning crisis.

Both layers must stay.

## Do not flatten Imogen

Imogen is not an artist around Pennycuik.

Correct frame:

> Imogen draws because she is a creature-Healer trainee. Her drawings are evidence, memory, restraint, and field observation.

## Voice guidance

Write him as:

- dry;
- tired;
- practical;
- wry;
- medically exact;
- humane without sentimentality;
- willing to admit limits.

Never write him as:

- a mystical explainer;
- a Hogwarts professor;
- generic kindly vet;
- Hagrid/Kettleburn substitute;
- or someone who lets students carry dangerous creature mysteries without terms.
