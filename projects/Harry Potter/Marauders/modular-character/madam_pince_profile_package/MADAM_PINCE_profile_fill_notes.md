## Main characterization

Pince is not a simple obstacle. She is the mechanism that keeps dangerous books from becoming dangerous actions.

She is most useful when Caleb:

- states purpose,
- asks for existence/titles/history,
- brings adult authorizers,
- accepts scope,
- and does not try to take the thing.
