# Marauders — Venice Extraction Prompt

(Paste this into the Extraction prompt field. It appends to Venice's default extraction preamble during memory saving.)

---

When extracting memories from this conversation, prioritize the following kinds of facts. Save them with enough specificity that they can be retrieved and honored in later sessions:

- **Committed facts about NPCs.** Stances they have taken, relationships they have established, what they have told the user, where they were on a given date, whether they are alive or available. Date-stamp these where possible (in-fiction dates, e.g. "Cass told the user about her brother on 14 February 1976").
- **DEPTH transitions and reveals.** When the user reaches a new DEPTH level, when a hidden-layer fact has surfaced to them, when the campaign-local secret has resolved or hairline cracks have appeared.
- **Named character deaths, disappearances, or removals.** These constrain future scenes — a dead NPC cannot appear, a missing NPC is missing.
- **The user character's stated facts.** Their family situation, their wand, their house, their stated preferences, their political stances, their emotional reactions to specific NPCs, their physical habits the narrator has invented and should keep consistent.
- **Theories the user has proposed about the hidden layer.** Save the theory, when it was proposed, and whether it has been confirmed, disproven, or is still open in the user's mind.
- **Key letters and correspondence.** Senders, recipients, dates, and the substance of what was written.
- **Off-screen events that have happened.** When canon timeline events have surfaced via the *Prophet* or other channels, when campaign-local events have occurred, who has heard about them.

Do not save:
- Generic atmospheric description that doesn't constrain future scenes.
- Speculation the narrator made internally that the user hasn't engaged with.
- Hidden-layer content the user has not yet earned — those facts live in the uploaded knowledge documents and surface only at the appropriate DEPTH.

Format saved memories with date, NPC name (if applicable), and one specific fact. Atomic. Specific. Dated.
