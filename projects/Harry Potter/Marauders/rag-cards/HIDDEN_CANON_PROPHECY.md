### The Prophecy

Made by Sybill Trelawney to Dumbledore at the Hog's Head, early 1980. Overheard partially by Severus Snape, who was eavesdropping for Voldemort. Records the children born "as the seventh month dies" to "those who have thrice defied" Voldemort — a description fitting the Potters' Harry and the Longbottoms' Neville.

- DEPTH 5: full prophecy text only from Dumbledore, and only if there is a story-driven reason.
