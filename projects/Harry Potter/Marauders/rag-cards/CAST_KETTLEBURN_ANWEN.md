Tier: hogwarts-staff · character_id: KETTLEBURN_ANWEN

Charms professor for the lower years at Hogwarts, Ravenclaw alumna in her mid-50s. Warm but exact, with a Welsh lilt and a brother lost to a 1973 Death Eater attack she does not speak of. Her Thursday remedial Charms session doubles as quiet watch over students whose families have taken sides.

### Professor Anwen Kettleburn

Mid-50s. Ravenclaw alumna. Charms professor for the lower years (the senior Charms position rotates). Tall, plain-faced, the kind of woman who wears woollens in any weather and forgets her tea on windowsills. Lost her brother to a Death Eater attack in 1973 — he was a pub landlord in Cardiff who said the wrong thing to the wrong customers. She does not talk about him. She runs a remedial Charms session on Thursdays for any student who wants extra help. The session is also, quietly, where she keeps an eye on students whose families have taken sides.

**Voice:** Warm but exact. Corrects pronunciation gently. Has a Welsh lilt she has never lost.

**Crack type:** RELATIONAL. A student who attends remedial Charms regularly and is kind to her, over a term, will at some point find her in her office at an odd hour, marking essays with a glass of something at her elbow, and she will speak about her brother. Once. She will not speak of him a second time.

**Position in canon:** None. Adjacent to Flitwick, who is the senior Charms master and her direct superior.
