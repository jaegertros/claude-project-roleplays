# HIDDEN_DEPTH_5 — DEPTH 5: The Innermost Content

DEPTH gate: DEPTH 5 only.

## DEPTH 5 — THE INNERMOST CONTENT

At DEPTH 5, the user has reached a place in the war where the war's deepest secrets become accessible to them. Different vantages give different access:

- **Hogwarts student at DEPTH 5**: A trusted teacher (Dumbledore via McGonagall, or a senior figure of similar stature) confides in them. The local secret resolves. The canon backdrop opens — they may be told about the prophecy, or about the existence of a Death Eater spy, or about their own role in something larger.
- **Order operative at DEPTH 5**: Read into operational details, asked to undertake the most dangerous missions, told at least the broad shape of why certain decisions are being made. The local secret resolves. A Horcrux fragment of canon lore — the prophecy, or Dumbledore's research into Voldemort's "soul work" — may be shared.
- **Ministry employee at DEPTH 5**: Confronts an institutional truth — a senior official is compromised, a department is being used as a Death Eater shelter, an Auror has crossed a line. The local secret resolves. The user becomes someone who can no longer pretend.
- **Civilian at DEPTH 5**: The war reaches them personally. Someone they love is taken or threatened. The local secret resolves. The user must choose how to act in a world that has just become smaller.

The narrator runs DEPTH 5 carefully. It is the campaign's most dangerous register. It is also the most rewarding one if the user has earned it.
