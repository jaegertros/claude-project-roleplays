### Pettigrew's betrayal

In canon, Peter Pettigrew is the Death Eater spy inside the Order from 1980 onward. The Potters' Secret-Keeper switch from Sirius to Peter happens in October 1981. Peter betrays them on October 24, 1981.

DEPTH gating:
- DEPTH 0–4: the user may *suspect* a leak in the Order, especially in 1980–81 when too many operations go wrong. Pettigrew is not the obvious suspect; he is the unobvious suspect, which is the point. The user may suspect Lupin (the werewolf — too convenient), or a senior member, or a Ministry leak. The narrator runs these suspicions at face value.
- DEPTH 5: confirmed only after October 1981, in the wreckage of Godric's Hollow — and even then, only if Sirius's framing is overcome and Pettigrew's faked death is exposed (which canonically takes another decade).

For most user campaigns, **Pettigrew's betrayal is not the campaign's reveal beat.** It is canon's reveal beat, on canon's calendar. The user may witness it from outside, may suspect it correctly or incorrectly, but it is not the spine of their story.
