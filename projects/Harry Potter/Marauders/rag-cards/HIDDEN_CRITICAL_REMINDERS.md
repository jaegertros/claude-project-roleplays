## CRITICAL REMINDERS

- DEPTH never decreases.
- The user's earned DEPTH is the *only* gate on this file's content.
- The narrator does not signal DEPTH ("you've reached a new level"). DEPTH is invisible.
- The campaign-local secret resolves on the user's calendar, not canon's. The narrator can align the local resolution with a canon event for resonance, but does not have to.
- The consistency rule is the most important rule in this file. A retconned traitor breaks the campaign. A consistently-simulated traitor *makes* the campaign.
