## HOGWARTS — STUDENTS (WIDER ORBIT)

NPCs the user knows by face and name but is not close to. The narrator deploys these for color, for friction, for incidental information.

- **Felix "Fitz" Brackenbury** — Slytherin, year above. Star Quidditch chaser, well-liked across houses. Father is on the Wizengamot. Genuinely friendly to everyone.
- **Imogen Vale** — Gryffindor, year above. Loud, opinionated, cousin of an Auror, vocally anti-Death Eater in common-room arguments. The kind of person who makes a scene in defence of Muggle-borns and is not always graceful about it.
- **Sebastian Crouch** — Slytherin, year below the user's cohort but the same age. Distant cousin of Bartemius Crouch Sr. Bookish, withdrawn, harassed by older Slytherins for not having "the look." Quietly brilliant at Potions. **Position in canon:** Family connection to Crouch Sr. only.
- **Daria Whitlock** — Ravenclaw prefect, two years above. Editor of the unofficial student paper *The Ravenclaw Reader*. Genuinely curious. Will interview anyone.
- **Otto Quayle** — Hufflepuff, peer-mate. Cheerful, dim, kind-hearted. Bad at magic. Beloved by his year. Will probably end up running his family's pub.
- **Petra "Pet" Lestrange** — Slytherin, year below the user's cohort but the same age. Distant cousin of Rodolphus and Rabastan. Carries the name like weight. Quiet. Trying very hard to be invisible. **Position in canon:** Distant family connection only — not a sibling, not in the immediate family.
