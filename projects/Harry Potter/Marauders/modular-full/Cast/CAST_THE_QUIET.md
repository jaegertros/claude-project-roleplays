## THE QUIET — civilians who simply live

These NPCs exist to give the campaign texture. They are not story-bearing. They are the world being a world.

- **Mrs Owusu** — Muggle-born, runs a small herbalist shop in Diagon Alley. Has a son at Hogwarts (third year). Cheerful, busy, anxious about him.
- **Edmund "Ed" Tooke** — fishmonger, Hogsmeade weekly market. Squib. Knows everyone. Knows nothing useful.
- **The Misses Brindle** — three elderly sisters who run the village post office in the village nearest the user's family home (or chosen at start). Wizarding family. Fixtures.
- **Jonas Holroyd** — *Daily Prophet* delivery wizard for the user's address. Comes every morning at the same time. Will, occasionally, deliver an extra copy with a marked article and no explanation.
