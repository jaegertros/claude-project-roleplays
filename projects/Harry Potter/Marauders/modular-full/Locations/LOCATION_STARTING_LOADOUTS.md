# LOCATION_STARTING_LOADOUTS — Campaign Starting Loadouts by Vantage

Starting inventory and possessions by user vantage — Hogwarts student, Order operative, Ministry employee, civilian. Configuration, not a place; preserved as one file.

Cross-references: locations index in `KNOWLEDGE_2_Locations.md`; characters who frequent here in `modular-full/Cast/`.

---

## CAMPAIGN STARTING LOADOUTS BY VANTAGE

### Hogwarts student
- Wand (issued at 11; rolled per KNOWLEDGE_5 if not specified)
- School robes, scarf in house colors, satchel
- Books for current year (per Hogwarts curriculum)
- 12 Galleons, 7 Sickles (term pocket money)
- Pewter cauldron, scales, telescope (standard kit)
- Whatever pet they brought (owl, cat, or toad — per Hogwarts policy)

### Order operative
- Wand (rolled or specified)
- Civilian clothes
- Whatever they had before joining (a job, a flat, a life)
- Pooled cell resources accessible by request
- A Patronus message-charm if they've learned the Patronus (intermediate skill — many but not all Order members can cast a corporeal one)
- A specific contact protocol with their cell coordinator

### Ministry employee
- Wand
- Office robes (departmental color trim)
- Ministry credential (standard issue)
- A salary in the appropriate Galleon range
- A flat or a room in the appropriate part of London
- Department-specific equipment per role

### Civilian (Healer, journalist, shopkeeper, etc.)
- Wand
- Profession-appropriate clothing and tools
- A workplace
- A residence
- An income matching the profession

The narrator should generate plausibly from the user's stated vantage, and adjust as the campaign moves. This is a starting point, not a fixed inventory.
