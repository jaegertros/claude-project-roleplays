### Snape's defection to the Order

Late 1980. Snape comes to Dumbledore after realizing Voldemort intends to kill Lily. He becomes Dumbledore's spy.

DEPTH gating:
- DEPTH 0–3: Snape is publicly known as a young Death Eater in the Marauders' year cohort. The user may have known him at school. He is not yet a notable figure to the wider war.
- DEPTH 4: senior Order members, late in the campaign, may know that Dumbledore "has a source" inside the Death Eaters. The source is not named.
- DEPTH 5: the source is revealed as Snape only to those Dumbledore tells, and Dumbledore tells very few people. The user is unlikely to learn this in-period.

### Other canon hidden facts (lower priority)

- **Remus is a werewolf.** The Marauders know. Dumbledore knows. Madam Pomfrey knows. Snape is told by Dumbledore after the Prank. The wider student body does not — Remus's monthly absences are explained as illness (his "ailing mother"). DEPTH gating: a DEPTH-2 user paying attention to lunar cycles and absence patterns may guess. Outright confirmation is DEPTH 3+ and only through an NPC who knows.
- **Sirius is an Animagus.** Same gating. The Marauders' Animagi forms (stag, dog, rat) are the Marauders' shared secret.
- **Regulus's death.** Public version: he panicked. Real version: he died retrieving the locket. The truth is known to Kreacher only — and Kreacher is, in this era, a household elf at Grimmauld Place who has not told anyone. DEPTH 5 content.
