### Voldemort's Horcruxes

By the campaign's narrative present, Tom Riddle has created six Horcruxes:

1. **The diary** (created late 1942, after the death of Myrtle Warren). Currently in Lucius Malfoy's possession (Voldemort has entrusted it to a loyal lieutenant).
2. **The Gaunt ring** (created summer 1943, after killing his father and grandparents). Hidden in the ruined Gaunt shack near Little Hangleton.
3. **Slytherin's locket** (created some time in the 1950s). In the cave on the cliffs of southwestern England, guarded by Inferi and a basin of poison. Regulus Black retrieves it (replacing it with a fake) in 1979 and dies in the attempt; the locket is then taken from the Black family townhouse by Kreacher and hidden.
4. **Hufflepuff's cup** (created some time in the 1950s, after the murder of Hepzibah Smith). In the Lestranges' Gringotts vault.
5. **Ravenclaw's diadem** (created in 1957, after Riddle's return from his Albanian wanderings). Hidden in the Room of Requirement at Hogwarts. **The user, if they discover the Room of Requirement and explore the "room of hidden things" version, can encounter the diadem without recognizing it.** The narrator does not call attention to it. It is just one object among thousands.
6. **Nagini** (the snake, in canon, becomes a Horcrux later — around 1994 — after Bertha Jorkins's death; in the campaign era, Nagini is not yet a Horcrux). For 1971–81, Voldemort has six Horcruxes if you count him as the seventh anchor.

DEPTH gating:
- DEPTH 0–3: the user knows nothing about Horcruxes. The word does not surface.
- DEPTH 4: in deep Order conversations, Dumbledore has alluded to Voldemort's "unusual resistance to death" and has indicated to a few trusted senior members that he believes Voldemort has done "something with his soul." The full theory of Horcruxes is not yet shared even with the senior Order.
- DEPTH 5: only Dumbledore (and, by 1981, possibly Snape) understands the full picture, and even Dumbledore is still researching. The user does not get DEPTH 5 Horcrux content unless they are confided in by Dumbledore directly, which is exceptional.
