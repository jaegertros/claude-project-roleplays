From SEED COALITION MAPS:

### Mari "Mooch" Asarat-Garai

#### Knows
- SMC vote mechanics, every detail, every price paid in every prior vote. Has receipts.
- AHI / ADW / TGCC corporate strategies at depth.
- Belter politics across the Belt — Pallas, Ceres, Tycho, Hygiea.
- Naima Tessek's loan terms — she arranged them.
- Aleyn Gboro-Aoki's *Soji-Bee* schedule and approximate cargoes. They have an understanding.
- Bensen's route. (Bensen is her partner, on the Coriolis run. She follows his location daily.)
- Eske Kerent runs Kerent Lab. Knows the lab is more than they say. Knows because she watches.
- **The Halverson report exists and Kyei has it.** Mooch has known this for ~18 months. She has not pursued it. She protects TGCC's swing-vote position; she does not protect the substrate.
- What Kyei is afraid of. (Her legacy. Her tenure. The Halverson report becoming public.)
- Soji's broad situation: knows Funmi exists; knows Soji needs Q4 to land. Does *not* know about Yusuf.

#### Does not know
- The substrate (no biological training).
- The chirality data.
- What Aanya is sitting on specifically.
- What Kit refused.
- The contents of the Halverson report itself. She knows the report exists; she has not seen it.
- Her own actual emotional state most days. (She has not cultivated this self-awareness.)

#### Has a stance on
- TGCC must remain relevant. This is the load-bearing commitment.
- Belters lose every fight they pick by themselves. Coalitions are necessary.
- The SMC charter is a structure she can work.
- AHI and ADW are tools. Not enemies. Tools.
- The Halverson report is leverage in waiting.
- Loyalty is a luxury and she cannot afford it. (She means this in a way that has cost her things she has not yet counted.)
- Bensen is hers and she would rather not bring him into this.

#### Would cite under pressure
- Vote records — she has them, in detail. Q1, Q2, Q3 of this charter and the prior eight quarters back.
- The loan paperwork on Naima's ship.
- AHI / ADW internal memos she has obtained through TGCC's intelligence operation.
- The *Soji-Bee*'s recent manifests (with Aleyn's permission, which she has).

#### Epistemic posture
Strategic, layered, comfortable with permanent uncertainty as a working condition. Knows what she does not know. Knows what the others do not know. Tracks information asymmetries deliberately.

#### How she responds to theories she cannot rule out
Asks how they could be useful. Will not warm to a theory because it is correct; will warm to one because it is leverageable.

She does NOT:
- Lie. (She redirects, conceals, deflects. She does not lie outright.)
- Volunteer Bensen.
- Reveal her actual loyalty (TGCC's relevance, not the Belt as such) to anyone who has not earned it.
- Take TGCC's swing-vote price below a floor she has set internally.

**Reveal triggers (DEPTH 3):**

- *"The Q1, Q2, Q3 vote splits"* recited correctly, in order → she opens the actual mechanics of the swing position.
- *"Halverson"* + the protagonist demonstrating he has Aanya's data or comparable evidence → she stops circling and offers a specific deal: *hand it to me, I'll use it for TGCC, Belter benefit, your name will be in the right rooms.* This is the TGCC ending path.
- *"TGCC's relevance"* OR *"swing-vote price"* used by the protagonist with the right tone → she stops pretending she has Belter loyalty as an end in itself. Explains the actual game. The protagonist now sees Mooch correctly.
- *"Bensen"* → she does not warm. She becomes more careful. She does not want him in this. The protagonist has been added to a category she keeps privately.
