# EVENT_DAY6_EL_DORADO — Day 6 — El Dorado

## Day 6 — El Dorado

### Spine

Travel day. Leksi flies the Promise from Ibẹrẹ to El Dorado — six hours. The protagonist meets Cortez. Evening at the El Dorado pavilion.

### Beats

- **0600** — Pre-flight. Crew prepares; Aanya stays at Ibẹrẹ for one more day with her samples.
- **0800** — Departure.
- **1400** — Arrival at El Dorado. The cliff-face landing. The view (the only constant sky on Shaula). The protagonist is met by an ADW liaison who walks him to Cortez's office.
- **1500** — Cortez's office. The maps. The handshake. The Mariner Valley drawl. Cortez is courteous, slightly puzzled by an Earther in Survey Corps, professionally interested. He invites the protagonist to dinner at the pavilion that evening.
- **1700** — End of formal day. Free time. The protagonist may visit Tariq's geological survey wing; Tariq is there.
- **1900** — Pavilion dinner. Cortez, possibly Marisol Halász-Tejeda (she's a regular), Vargas at the bar. Real coffee, real wood smell, terraforming songs. Cortez talks about the MAG project. He shows photos of his sons. He does not ask about the protagonist's Earth.
- **2030** — Sister Gabriela's evening prayer in the corner. Five attendees. Quiet.
- **2200** — Cortez retires. The pavilion stays open. Marisol challenges the protagonist to *Conde* if he expressed interest.
- **2300** — End of evening. Sleep in ADW guest quarters.

### Aanya's morning

Aanya, alone at Ibẹrẹ, runs the protagonist's Day 5 sample through her own protocols. The result confirms what she has been seeing for six weeks. She sends him a message at 1100 — *"We need to talk. Tomorrow when you can."* This sets up Day 7's Halverson-question opening.

### What's off-screen

Mooch monitors the protagonist's movement; she has a TGCC observer at El Dorado who will pass back a report on his evening at the pavilion. Soji is in Ibẹrẹ; she does not interact today. Kyei is in Ibẹrẹ.

### Decision points

- Whether to accept Cortez's drink offer.
- How to engage with Marisol if she shows up.
- Whether to visit Sister Gabriela's prayer.
