# LOCATION_KERENT_LAB — Kerent Lab

Eske Kerent's independent UN-charter research outpost in the Iji foothills — twelve staff, the unofficial Halverson sample bunker, professionally grudging coexistence with the SMC.

## 7. Kerent Lab

Eske Kerent's research outpost in the Iji range foothills, ~40 km from Ibẹrẹ along a maintained corporate road. A small modular complex — three habitat modules, two research labs, a sample-storage bunker built into the rock, a vehicle bay. ~12 staff total. UN-charter independent research permit, grudgingly tolerated by the SMC.

### 7.1 The Lab Approach

`location: kerent_approach`

A turn-off from the AHI Iji-range maintenance road, marked with a small UN-research-charter sign. The road becomes gravel for the last 2 km. The complex is visible only when you crest a low rise; otherwise it's tucked into a small valley.

**Sensory anchor:** Sudden quiet (the wind is broken by the valley), the smell of cold Iji air without the AHI complex's thermal exhaust haze.

**Who's here:** Two security staff at the gate. Eske personally meets visitors of consequence.

**Access:** UN charter permit holders, scheduled visitors. The protagonist can request access via SMC inter-charter protocols; Eske will accept.

### 7.2 The Main Lab

`location: kerent_lab`

Working microbiology lab, well-equipped for its size. Pavel Adamec's domain when he is in (he is also at the field sites half the week). Centrifuge, chemistry deck, sample microscopes, sequencers. A whiteboard with hand-drawn organism phylogenies, half of them with question marks.

**Sensory anchor:** The whiteboard. The honest uncertainty of the marks.

**Who's here:** Pavel, daily. Eske, often. Two graduate-student-equivalent juniors.

**Access:** Lab staff and invited visitors. Aanya has visited once; the protagonist should, eventually.

### 7.3 The Sample Storage Bunker

`location: kerent_bunker`

A small, climate-controlled storage facility built into the rock. Holds the lab's accumulated specimen collection — ~3,000 indexed samples, some in cryo, some refrigerated, some in stable preservatives. The unofficial copy of the partial-leak Halverson report fragments is here, on a sealed datacore, in the back row.

**Sensory anchor:** Cold air, the soft hum of cooling systems, the faint smell of preservative.

**Access:** Eske and Pavel only. Eske will show the protagonist the bunker at *Teaming Up* tier or higher.
