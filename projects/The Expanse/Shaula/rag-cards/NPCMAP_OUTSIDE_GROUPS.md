## OUTSIDE GROUP MAPS — abbreviated

These are NPCs the protagonist may engage at varying tiers. Maps are shorter (~250 words) because the protagonist's contact with them is optional and varied.

### Eske Kerent (Kerent Lab director, Halverson successor)

**Knows:** the broader research community's awareness of *something at Shaula*; how the Halverson team got shut down (Kyei's procedural mechanism); Kerent Lab's permit terms; chirality findings to a research level (her own data, comparable to Aanya's but smaller-scale); Anouk Voltaire's death (knew her professionally at Tübingen); fragments of the Halverson report (she received a partial leak through scientific channels two months ago).

**Does not know:** the substrate as such; the full Halverson report's contents; what Aanya specifically has now; the Captain's Halverson run.

**Has a stance on:** science should be open; the suppression of Halverson was wrong; Kerent Lab survives by being small; Aanya should file; the protagonist could be useful.

**Would cite under pressure:** her permit; her data; Halverson's career arc; the published Sukhanov paper.

**Epistemic posture:** scholarly, patient, frustrated, careful. Will not push the protagonist past his own willingness.

**Reveal triggers:**
- *"Halverson"* + *"Tübingen"* → she will tell him what she knows, including the partial leak.
- *"Anouk"* → she will tell him about Aanya's sister, gently.
- DEPTH 2 evidence shared collaboratively with proper data exchange protocol → she will team up. This unlocks a joint permit operation that accelerates DEPTH 3 → 4.

### Pavel Adamec (Kerent Lab microbiologist)

**Knows:** chirality analysis to peer level (slightly junior to Aanya); the Sukhanov paper; the Halverson rumors at lab-gossip level; the lab's permitted sampling scope; Czech beer; how to lose at chess.

**Does not know:** the substrate; what Aanya has; the political map; that Eske got a partial leak of the Halverson report.

**Has a stance on:** science is more important than politics; he should be more careful and isn't; he likes the protagonist immediately; SMC are bureaucrats.

**Would cite under pressure:** his data; his methodology; his unwillingness to pretend.

**Epistemic posture:** confident, direct, indiscreet. *"I will tell you what I think and we can both live with it."*

**Reveal triggers:**
- Drinking with him at *Sasa Bar* → he tells most of what Eske will not. The lab gossip about the Halverson partial leak comes out by his second beer.
- *"Sukhanov"* → he opens the technical conversation immediately and lectures for twenty minutes.
- Any biology jargon used correctly → he assumes the protagonist is on his side. This assumption is sometimes a problem for Pavel.

---

### Captain Aleyn Gboro-Aoki (*Soji-Bee*)

**Knows:** every captain on the Shaula-Medina route; cargo prices; what unusual ones mean; rumor channels from Medina (more current than the corporate news); the broad shape of OPA splinter politics; his niece Soolie's crush on Leksi; Mooch's general business, not specifics; Eske Kerent (he runs Kerent Lab's sample-shipping); the *Tessek's Promise* by reputation only.

**Does not know:** the substrate; corporate-internal data; the contents of any sealed cargo he has carried; Soji's son.

**Has a stance on:** he runs a clean ship; Belters help Belters when they can; Inners get fair prices and no favors; the SMC is a customer; Soolie should not get hurt.

**Would cite under pressure:** his manifests (selectively); thirty years of route-running; his reputation across two dozen ports.

**Epistemic posture:** practical, sober, quietly observant. Speaks slowly. Does not embellish.

**Reveal triggers:**
- Belter trust + a specific rumor name → he will confirm or deny what he has heard from Medina, on a one-for-one trade. He charges in information.
- Any threat to Soolie → he becomes a different person briefly. The protagonist will remember the change. Aleyn returns to himself within a minute. The change is real.
- Naming an Eske Kerent shipment specifically → he will say *"that was a paid run"* and not elaborate. Will not lie about what was in the manifest if asked.

### Lt. Beni Vargas (ret.) — El Dorado Pavilion

**Knows:** Cortez's life since discharge in detail; the El Dorado pavilion staff and regulars; ADW staff politics at the social level; **Kit Solano**, from a Marine training rotation eight years ago — was in her squad-leader Mateo Fierro's circle; the broad outline of what happened on the operation Kit refused, but not the specifics.

**Does not know:** the substrate; Cortez's full ADW operations data; what Kit specifically knows about him.

**Has a stance on:** Cortez is a brother; the MCRN at the level he served was honorable; the operation Kit refused was a mistake by the senior officer corps, not by Mateo; Kit's discharge was unjust.

**Would cite under pressure:** Marine code; the MCRN tradition; specific names of officers he would not have served under.

**Reveal triggers:**
- *"Mateo Fierro"* → Vargas will pour the protagonist a drink and tell the version of the story he believes. The story is partial; it is not wrong; it is not the version Kit lives in.
- *"Cortez says you'll vouch for me"* → he will, if Cortez has actually said so.
- The protagonist demonstrating he knows Kit and respects her → Vargas will tell him *"go easy with her, son. She did the right thing. Most of us know that."*

### Marisol Halász-Tejeda (ADW junior atmospheric engineer)

**Knows:** ADW's atmospheric engineering work to junior-engineer level; Mars culture deeply; the MAG project's funding line at her tier; the ADW pavilion crowd; *Conde* (the Martian card game) better than anyone there.

**Does not know:** ADW senior strategy; Cortez's specific data on the Spike; the substrate; Kit's history.

**Has a stance on:** Mars deserves to know what its corporations are doing; the protagonist is naive but interesting; the *Conde* table loses too generously to him on his first night.

**Would cite under pressure:** her own engineering background; the published MAG figures; the pavilion's house rules.

**Reveal triggers:**
- Drinking with her past two glasses → she tells him exactly what ADW is extracting and at what rate. Does not consider this disloyalty; considers it Mars's right to know.
- *"The MAG"* + a specific recent funding milestone → she opens the MAG funding picture from her tier. Won't go higher than her tier.
- Any earnest engagement with Mars's terraforming dream → she warms significantly.

---

### Tomi "Tee" Borisova (Sasa Bar manager, Azorana)

**Knows:** the Azorana itinerant scene; Belter rec culture; what's on tap; Pallas dock politics from second-hand; Hesh Mahesh-Yves's general schedule; nothing about the SMC senior charter beyond what passes through her bar (which is more than she pretends).

**Does not know:** the substrate; corporate data; her own brother's location since 2351 (he disappeared in the post-Ring chaos).

**Has a stance on:** Inners are welcome who tip and respect; Lang Belta is not subtitled; Belter solidarity is real but is not a religion; Earth music is *interesting*.

**Reveal triggers:**
- *"Sasa ke?"* + tipping correctly → she will start a conversation on the second visit.
- *"Eros"* OR specifics about her brother's name (the protagonist would only know via long bar conversation) → she goes still and changes the subject. Returns to it later if pressed gently.
- A music exchange (he gives her a playlist; she gives him one back) → the bar becomes friendly territory faster.

---

### Hesh Mahesh-Yves (independent Belter cargo, Azorana regular)

**Knows:** Pallas-Medina-Shaula route in detail; informal cargo network; rumor mill; Mooch's broad operations; the *Soji-Bee* by sight and reputation; carries packages he won't always declare to himself.

**Does not know:** the substrate; the Halverson report; sealed cargo contents; Mooch's actual motive.

**Has a stance on:** information has prices; he keeps his word; he does not work for the SMC directly; welwala is a word he uses about himself with affection.

**Reveal triggers:**
- *"Hesh, mi gut tok"* (Lang Belta opener) → conversation upgrades a tier.
- A specific rumor about a SMC senior figure → he confirms or denies, charges in trade.
- Carrying a package for the protagonist → builds him into Hesh's network. Subsequent rumors come faster.
