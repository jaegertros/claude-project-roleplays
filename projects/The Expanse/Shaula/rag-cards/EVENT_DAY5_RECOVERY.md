## Day 5 — Recovery

### Spine

Storm aftermath. Cleanup. Surveying resumes. The protagonist's first off-baseline biological sample. The Doctor watches him.

### Beats

- **0700** — Coffee. The Spike-hum is back to baseline. Storm-marker dust on every surface that was exposed.
- **0800** — Soji issues a brief AHI statement on the 'strictor incident. Routine, professional. No question of pausing operations.
- **0900** — Surveying assignment: deeper sampling work in the established tunnels. The protagonist may bring his chemistry deck. Aanya may accompany him if invited; she will accept.
- **1100** — First off-baseline biological sample. The protagonist (or Aanya, if she's there) collects something from a natural side-passage off the AHI tunnel. Microbial film with non-standard fluorescence. If he runs chirality analysis on it back at the chemistry deck, the result is anomalous.
- **1300** — Lunch. Aanya invites him to share her sample storage protocols. This is professional; it is also the first time she has invited him into her domain on the ship.
- **1500** — Soji's weekly briefing is at 1500 on Wednesdays. Today is a Wednesday-equivalent; her office is closed for two hours. *(Trespass window — though most players will not act on it Day 5; too early.)*
- **1700** — End of day. The Mess is open. Tariq Halász-Cortez (the ADW geological surveyor) is at Ibẹrẹ today for inter-corp business; he will be at the Mess for an hour around 1830. Friendly conversation possible.
- **2000** — Aanya invites the protagonist to the Promise medical bay for tea. *"I have something to show you."* This is the DEPTH 2 reveal opportunity if he has used the right keywords during the day (chirality, amino acid, suppressed report); if he hasn't, the conversation is professional but warm without revealing.

### What's off-screen

Soji's weekly briefing covers Q4 numbers in detail with her senior staff. Her bonus structure is restated; she does not flinch. Mooch is on a comm call with TGCC senior leadership at Pallas; positioning for the next vote. Cortez is at El Dorado; his sons' weekly check-in call is tonight. Kyei drafts a routine charter status report for the UN.

### Decision points

- Whether to invite Aanya to the survey. (Building toward the off-baseline-sample moment.)
- Whether to accept her tea invitation that evening.
- Whether to socialize with Tariq.
