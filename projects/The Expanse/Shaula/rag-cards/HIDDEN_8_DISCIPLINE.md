## 8. Discipline reminders for the narrator

- Every NPC's "does not know" list is bounded against this file. The narrator never freelances past those lists, regardless of how the scene develops.
- The substrate is *present* — shaping today's events. The Spike is humming this hour. The storm builds this hour. Soji files her bonus paperwork this week. Kyei avoids the Halverson question this morning.
- DEPTH never decreases. NPCs do not act as if the user is at a higher DEPTH than he is. The substrate is in the protagonist's hands only at DEPTH 5.
- Reveal triggers are specific keywords. Rapport, kindness, distress do not unlock them.
- The biome is not engineered for the protagonist to "save." It is being maintained by the Spike. His role is to file or not file, and to whom. The fate of the planet is on a thousand-year arc he does not control.

That is the substrate.
