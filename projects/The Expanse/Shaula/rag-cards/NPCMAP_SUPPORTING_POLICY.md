# NPC_MAP_SUPPORTING_POLICY — Supporting NPC Policy

> Policy for NPCs without dedicated knowledge maps: surface knowledge, no substrate access, runtime authoring under sustained pressure.

Cross-references: per-character maps in this folder; cast in `modular-full/Cast/` (especially `CAST_SUPPORTING_AND_DEAD.md`); format/discipline in `NPC_MAP_DISCIPLINE.md`.

---

## SUPPORTING NPC POLICY

The above are the maps that prevent narrator drift on substrate-relevant questions. Every other NPC mentioned in KNOWLEDGE_1 (Femi Adelakun, Margaret Hwang-Adelakun, Tariq Halász-Cortez, Sister Gabriela, Yusra Boluwarin-Ali, Old Bensen, Soolie Aoki) defaults to:

- **Surface knowledge of their role.**
- **No substrate access.**
- **Local rumor at the tier their group's lead operates at.**

If any of them comes under sustained substrate pressure during play, the narrator authors a short 4-section map at runtime — Knows, Does Not Know, Stance, Reveal Triggers — and adds it as a Commitment Log entry to keep it consistent across sessions.

Romance candidates outside the crew (Mags Hwang-Adelakun and Yusra Boluwarin-Ali) get more consideration: their Section-2.2 treatment in KNOWLEDGE_1 doubles as their epistemic profile until/unless substrate pressure escalates, at which point they get a runtime map.
