# LOCATION_AZORANA — The Azorana — TGCC's flotilla flagship

A converted ice-hauler in geosynchronous orbit around Lesath, now TGCC's Shaula operations hub and the protagonist's Day 1 arrival point.

Cross-references: locations index in `KNOWLEDGE_2_Locations.md`; related Cast in `modular-full/Cast/`; related Events in `modular-full/Events/`.

## 4. The Azorana — TGCC's flotilla flagship

A converted 60-year-old ice-hauler, ~800 m long, ~100 m wide at the beam, in geosynchronous orbit around Lesath. Originally built to drag iceteroids from the Saturnian rings to Mars; converted by TGCC after the Belt's purchase of distressed UN assets in 2348. Now houses TGCC's Shaula operations, ~600 staff, and a constant rotation of itinerant Belter labor that doubles the population on busy weeks.

The Azorana is the protagonist's first arrival point on the Day 1 orbital approach. The Day 2 SMC briefing happens here.

### 4.1 The Approach and Dock

`location: azorana_dock`

The exterior of the Azorana is a working ship that stopped working: ice-grippers retracted but not removed, drag-tow rigging still bolted to the hull because removing it would cost more than leaving it. TGCC has painted the upper third in charcoal-and-gold, leaving the lower hull in the original UN gray. From space it looks like a decommissioned thing inhabited.

**Sensory anchor on docking:** The dock cycle is rougher than corporate-quality (TGCC saves on dampers); the airlock smells of welding from a recent repair; the light is warmer than Inner-corporate (Belter preference, lower Kelvin).

**Who's here:** Dockworkers (Belter, Lang Belta dominant). A TGCC desk attendant who checks credentials and is always Hesh Mahesh-Yves's cousin. Sometimes Mooch is on the dock; sometimes Aleyn from the *Soji-Bee* is, if his ship is in.

**Access:** Public to anyone with TGCC clearance. SMC contracts grant clearance.

### 4.2 The Bridge / Command Level

`location: azorana_command`

Mooch's domain. Three holographic vote-tally displays dominate the back wall, each tracking different SMC voting cycles in real time. Mooch's office is off the main command floor; her door is rarely closed but the implication of attention is constant.

**Sensory anchor:** The displays' soft blue-orange light against the matte black of the command room walls; the absolute quiet (Mooch demands it).

**Who's here:** Mooch, almost always. Her senior staff — three TGCC officers whose names the protagonist will not learn unless he engages at *Business* tier with TGCC.

**Access:** SMC charter business only. The protagonist enters here for the Day 2 briefing.

### 4.3 The Sasa Bar (Recreation Bay 4)

`location: azorana_sasa_bar`

The unofficial bar, two decks down from command. Tomi "Tee" Borisova's domain. Large enough for forty people seated, sixty if they don't mind standing. Bar along one wall, mismatched tables and chairs throughout, a small stage that gets used about twice a week for live music. OPA news streams on a screen at low volume. Lang Belta is the dominant language; standard English is heard but not the default.

**Sensory anchors:** Belter beer — slightly metallic, slightly bitter, brewed in a TGCC cooperative recycling tank that Belters trust and Inners do not. The constant overlap of conversations in two registers. Tee's signature greeting *"Sasa ke?"* every time someone new walks in (the joke and the actual greeting at once).

**Who's here:** Itinerant Belter crews on layover. TGCC junior staff. Hesh Mahesh-Yves is here ~3 evenings a week. Pavel Adamec drinks here twice a week (he is the only regular Inner). Tee is always behind the bar.

**Access:** Public. Inners are tolerated; tipping correctly and not ordering "lite" beer is the entry requirement to be welcomed beyond once.

**Function in arc:** Primary Belter social space. The drinking-buddies tier with Hesh, with Pavel, and with the regulars happens here.

### 4.4 The TGCC Archive

`location: azorana_archive`

A small, well-organized records room two levels below command. Mooch's vote-record ledger lives here, in a sealed datacore — more honest than the official charter minutes filed with the UN. Includes prices paid in every quarter's votes since Q1 of the charter.

**Sensory anchor:** Cool air (the datacore is climate-controlled), absolutely silent except for the hum of the cores; a single soft amber light overhead.

**Who's here:** Restricted. A TGCC archivist named Yusra Boluwarin-Ali (the romance pool TGCC tech) has access. She enters once a day to verify integrity. Mooch enters when she needs to consult.

**Access:** TGCC senior only, which the protagonist is not. **DEPTH 4 trespass affordance.** Entry without authorization requires either Yusra's collusion (a relationship investment) or a security workaround (Kit can advise).

### 4.5 The Itinerant Berths

`location: azorana_itinerant_berths`

The Azorana's outer ring docking slips, where transient ships tie up. The *Soji-Bee* is here when in port. A handful of independent Belter haulers, occasional UN-charter ships. Less formal than the main dock — closer to a dock-town than an entry point.

**Sensory anchor:** The smell of working ships — fuel, hot metal, food cooking in galleys with the airlocks open. Music from somewhere; the *Soji-Bee* plays Belter-dialect Mormon hymns at evening services.

**Who's here:** Captains and their crews. Aleyn Gboro-Aoki and Soolie when the *Soji-Bee* is in. Various transient faces. Hesh Mahesh-Yves's ship, when he's in.

**Access:** Anyone who has business there. The protagonist finds his way here naturally if he's looking to talk to Aleyn.
