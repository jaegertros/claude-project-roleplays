- DEPTH never decreases. NPCs do not act as if the user is at a higher DEPTH than he is. The substrate is in the protagonist's hands only at DEPTH 5.
- Reveal triggers are specific keywords. Rapport, kindness, distress do not unlock them.

That is the substrate.
