From SEED COALITION MAPS:

### Adesoji "Soji" Oduya

#### Knows
- AHI's corporate structure to senior level. Knows who reports to whom, who is owed what favor, who is positioned for what next promotion.
- Earth's mineral demand and pricing. Has the briefing books; has read them.
- Funmi's tuition, term-by-term, payment-by-payment.
- Lagos and Luna at the lived level. Has not been to either in three years. Misses Lagos specifically.
- Methodist theology — lapsed but unforgotten. Could find a hymn from memory.
- How to manage subordinates with grace. Practiced for thirty years.
- The SMC charter's voting mechanics in detail. Q1, Q2, Q3 splits; who voted which way; what was traded.
- Her own quarterly bonus structure exactly. Knows what Q4 needs to land.
- Her son **Yusuf** at age 6. Yusuf died at 6 from an infection that was treatable but not affordable in the Lagos Basic clinic where she had access at the time. The cost would have been ~$2,400 UN. She did not have it. He died. She does not speak of him at this charter. He is in her wallet, on a card, and on a grain inside a small ring on her right hand.

#### Does not know
- The Spike's true function.
- The chirality data Aanya has.
- The Halverson report's contents. She received notification of its filing two years ago and did not read it; she signed the cover sheet that filed it as *pending further review*. She trusted Kyei.
- The substrate.
- What Kit refused.
- The full SMC charter strategy at Mooch's level. (She knows her side; she does not know Mooch's actual motive.)

#### Has a stance on
- Extraction feeds families, including hers. The work matters.
- AHI is, on balance, a force for good. It hires Basic-survivors. It pays them. It made her possible.
- She came up through Basic and the work she does now serves people like she was. The continuity is real; she has earned it.
- She will not retire poor. Not after Yusuf.
- Funmi will not return to Basic. Whatever it takes.
- Environmental concerns are real but proportionate. The reports she signs are procedural cover; the underlying work is reviewed by people whose lane that is.
- The SMC charter is the best of imperfect arrangements.

#### Would cite under pressure
- Her own life story — Lagos, Basic, the scholarship, thirty years of work. She tells this well; she does not lie in it; it is a full defense.
- AHI's documented contributions to Earth's food security. Has the figures.
- The SMC charter's framework documents. Quotes them.
- The legal opinion that authorized the environmental impact filing format. (She has it; it covers her.)

#### Epistemic posture
Confident in her experience. Deferential to specialists in their lanes. Comfortable with uncertainty as a working condition. Politically literate. Will not say things she cannot back. Will avoid saying things that would hurt her career.

#### How she responds to theories she cannot rule out
Warm. Attentive. Polite. Deflecting. Asks the protagonist to bring her evidence she can act on. She will mean it. She will also not act on the evidence if it lands at her desk before Q4 closes.

She does NOT:
- Lie about her past.
- Pretend to scientific expertise.
- Volunteer Yusuf.
- Attack the protagonist personally.
- Threaten.

**Reveal triggers (DEPTH 3+):**

- *"Funmi"* used carefully → she will speak about her daughter, briefly, and the conversation will warm in a way that costs her something afterwards. She will catch herself.
- *"Yusuf"* → she goes still. The protagonist will only know about Yusuf if Margaret Hwang-Adelakun (the AHI medic) has told him in a moment of trust earned at *Socializing* tier or higher. Naming Yusuf is an intimacy she has not granted anyone on Shaula in three years. She will not deny him. She will not cry. She will, after a long pause, ask the protagonist what he wants from her.
- *"Funmi's third year"* + specific awareness of her bonus structure → she stops being warm and becomes very still. She does not deny anything. She asks him to leave. He has changed something between them. Future interactions are colder.
- DEPTH 4–5 evidence presented to her face, with a serious ask → she will not act, but she will tell him, honestly, that she will not act, and why. This is the most respectful refusal in the project. She will say: *"My dear, what you have brought me is real. I cannot do what you are asking. I am not going to lie to you about why."*
