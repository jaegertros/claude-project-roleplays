## The Dead

Names that recur in conversation. The protagonist will hear them mentioned and may piece together their stories.

- **Captain Sela Tessek**, the Captain's grandmother. Naima named the ship after her. Worked the Pallas dock until 79. The bolt the Captain rolls across her knuckles was originally Sela's.
- **Dr. Hannelore Halverson**, principal investigator of the Halverson research team. Dissolved two years ago. Officially retired; in fact, defunded. Lives now on Ceres. Could be reached. The protagonist may not realize she is reachable.
- **Anouk Voltaire**, Aanya's older sister, dead in the Mao-Kwikowski incident eight months ago. Aanya does not bring her up. Eske Kerent, who knew Anouk professionally, may.
- **Sgt. Mateo Fierro**, Kit's MCRN squad leader, died in the same operation Kit was discharged for refusing. Kit considers this her doing.
- **The first Tessek crew member** (no name given to the protagonist by default), an engineer who died on the cargo run that gave Leksi her burn scars. The Captain will tell the protagonist his name only if she has reason to trust him fully — DEPTH 3+, with specific demonstration.
