## Day 7 — Halverson surfaces (CRITICAL — hour-by-hour)

### Spine

The Halverson name enters the protagonist's awareness via three possible paths: (a) Aanya's data conversation; (b) the Captain mentions her old run; (c) Eske Kerent reaches out via formal letter to the SMC. At least one of these three triggers today. Kyei is available for interview if the protagonist requests one. Eske will host him at Kerent Lab if he asks.

### Hour-by-hour

#### 0700
Aanya's message from yesterday evening. She wants to talk. The protagonist is at El Dorado; he can comm her.

#### 0800
Comm call with Aanya (if accepted). She tells him: *"I have been sitting on data for six weeks. I want to show it to you. Not over comm. When you're back at Ibẹrẹ or on the ship."* This is a DEPTH 2 → 3 trigger if the protagonist asks the right follow-up — *non-standard chirality?* or *amino acid mismatch?* or *suppressed report?* — she opens at the peer level.

#### 0900
A formal letter arrives in the SMC system, addressed to the protagonist by name. From Dr. Eske Kerent, Kerent Lab. Polite, professional. Requests a meeting at his earliest convenience to discuss "scientific findings of mutual interest pertaining to the Iji-range biome." The letter does not name Halverson directly. It does not need to.

#### 1000
The protagonist's morning at El Dorado. Optional: Cortez's morning briefing if invited. Tariq is at his lab; conversation possible.

#### 1100
If the protagonist asks Cortez about Halverson directly (a DEPTH 2 trigger requiring the name), Cortez says: *"Halverson? I remember the name. Independent UN team, ran on Iji range two years back. Shut down. Don't recall the why. Soji might know — it was on her side."* This is genuine, not deflection. Cortez does not know.

#### 1200
Lunch. The protagonist may accept a comm meeting with Eske for tomorrow (Day 8) or push to today; Eske can meet today at 1700 if he flies to Ibẹrẹ.

#### 1330
If the protagonist comms the Captain to ask whether they can arrange a Promise flight to Ibẹrẹ on short notice (to meet Eske today), the Captain can do this. Leksi flies. Six hours one way. He arrives at Kerent Lab at 1900 — late, but Eske waits.

#### 1500
**Alternate path A:** If the protagonist is still at El Dorado, the Captain comms him for a routine check-in. During the call, in the course of asking how the El Dorado work is going, the Captain mentions in passing: *"Two years back I ran a contract for a research team out there — Halverson? Eske Kerent's outfit before they got the boot. Sealed cargo. Didn't ask. Wonder what was in it sometimes."* This is the Captain's DEPTH 3 reveal *if* the protagonist has been demonstrating substrate-investigation effort; she is seeding the question. If the protagonist has not been investigating, the Captain does not mention it.

#### 1500 (alternate path B)
**Alternate path B:** If the protagonist has been to Sasa Bar regularly and built a rapport with Pavel Adamec (Kerent Lab microbiologist), Pavel is at Sasa today and will be drunk by 1700. He'll comm-message the protagonist with: *"We should talk. Properly. The Halverson stuff is not what they say it is."* This is Pavel's reveal — not gated by a keyword, gated by relational tier (Drinking Buddies / Inside).

#### 1700
If the protagonist is at El Dorado, Cortez has another offer — dinner with him tonight, more personal. If the protagonist is en route to Ibẹrẹ, the Promise is in transit. If he's at Sasa Bar, Pavel is buying.

#### 1900
If meeting Eske: she walks him through what she knows. The leak fragments of the Halverson report. The chirality findings. The biome-health gradients near mining shafts. Eske does not have the full report. Eske wants the protagonist's data in exchange for hers. This is *Teaming Up* tier — they sign the data-exchange agreement.

#### 2100
End of Day 7. The Halverson question is now live. The protagonist's DEPTH is at minimum 2, likely 3 if he engaged with one of the three paths.

### What's off-screen

Mooch monitors. She knows Eske sent a letter — TGCC's intelligence net flags formal SMC correspondence. She does not yet know what's been said. Kyei is in Ibẹrẹ; she has no reason to intervene unless the protagonist requests her time. Soji is in her office until 2100, signing routine paperwork.

### Decision points

- Whether to engage Aanya's data conversation today.
- Whether to accept Eske's meeting and on what timeline.
- Whether to ask Cortez or the Captain about Halverson.
- (If the player has built it) Whether to drink with Pavel.

DEPTH 2 → 3 reachable today via any of the three paths.
