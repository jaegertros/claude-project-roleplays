## What this file is

The 11-day arc from Medina Day 0 through Shaula Day 10. Day-by-day spine; hour-by-hour on the critical days (Day 4, Day 7, Day 9, Day 10). Off-screen activity catalog. Decision points named.

Per template Section 2.5: **scripted beats happen regardless of player action.** If the player does nothing on Day 4, the storm peaks; the 'strictor incident occurs; Femi files his report. If the player intervenes, the incident still occurs; the intervention has its own cost. The project's ethics live in how it handles consequences the player cannot prevent.

The protagonist can fail, refuse, or absent himself from any beat. The world continues without him. That is the rule.
