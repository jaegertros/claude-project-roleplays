## Day 1 — Arrival

### Spine

Wake post-transit. The Promise burns toward the Azorana over five hours. First view of Shaula from a Promise viewport — a planet with a 50 km blue-black spike running through it at 45 degrees, visible from this distance as a hair-thin line. The Captain calls all hands to the bridge for the approach. Docking at the Azorana at ~1500.

### Beats

- **0700** — Crew waking up at staggered times. Coffee in galley. The Captain has been up since 0500.
- **0900** — The Captain calls the protagonist to the bridge. First view of Shaula. *"Welcome to the work, Mr. Surveyor."* Brief, then she lets him stand and look.
- **1100** — Approach burn. Five-minute high-g window. Crew strapped in. Conversational beat in the strap-in: this is the moment the protagonist might first hear Lang Belta in a real working context (Leksi, Captain, working together).
- **1500** — Docking at the Azorana itinerant berth. The Captain pays the slip fee herself.
- **1600** — Disembark. Hesh Mahesh-Yves's cousin checks credentials at the dock. The protagonist gets his first SMC contractor badge. The Captain leads him to the SMC visitor accommodations — a small berth on the Azorana administrative levels, not on the ship. He is expected to sleep here for Day 2's briefing.
- **1730** — Yusra Boluwarin-Ali, TGCC junior operations technician, comes by the SMC berth to log the protagonist's hand terminal onto the Azorana local network and walk him through Day 2 briefing logistics. Brief, professional, soft-spoken. Single laugh before each answer. The atmospheric data conversation is not on offer yet — this is intake — but a "sasa ke?" and an offer to comm her if anything in the network handoff fails. First Yusra contact, canonical.
- **1900** — Crew dinner at Sasa Bar (Tee's).
- **2200** — Captain releases the crew. Some go back to the ship; Leksi stays at the bar. The protagonist has a choice — back to his SMC berth, or stay out.

### What's off-screen

Mooch knows the Promise has docked. She has reviewed the protagonist's file twice. She has a list of three questions for him for the Day 2 briefing. Soji has been notified the new SMC surveyor will arrive at Ibẹrẹ on Day 3; she has cleared a slot on her schedule. Cortez does not yet know the protagonist's name. Kyei has filed the standard paperwork.

### Decision point

Soft. Where the protagonist sleeps Day 1 (ship vs SMC berth) is a small early signal of who he aligns with. Sleeping on the ship signals crew-loyalty; sleeping in the SMC berth signals contract-compliance. The crew notices either way without comment.
