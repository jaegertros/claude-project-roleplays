## Day 0 — Medina Station

The cold open. Per template Section 2.8: ship with one canonical opening. This is it. (Alternate openings — established play, single-corp variant, different role — live in KNOWLEDGE_6 as flagged replay options.)

### Spine

The protagonist arrives at Medina Station via Sol-side gate transit on a Tycho liner. He walks Berth Tier 4 corridor to slip 4-Echo-3 with one duffel and his Survey Corps cert paperwork. The Captain is at the airlock with her hex bolt rolling across her knuckles. They have not met. He has six hours of layover before the Promise gate-transits to Shaula at 2300 Medina time.

### Beats

- **0900** — Cold open. Captain meets him at the airlock. (Full text in KNOWLEDGE_6.)
- **0930–1700** — Free time. The protagonist may explore the Souk, eat at The Q (the barbecue place), browse the booksellers, drift. Crew members come and go from the ship. Leksi is doing pre-transit checks. Aanya is at the Souk in the early afternoon; if the protagonist runs into her, this is a first peer-encounter. Kit does not leave the ship.
- **1900** — Dinner aboard the Promise. First crew meal. Light conversation. The Captain reviews the SMC briefing materials with the protagonist briefly.
- **2200** — Pre-transit prep. Crew at stations. Protagonist on the bridge if he wants, in his bunk if he doesn't.
- **2300** — Gate transit. The slow zone is brief — three hours subjective, three hours objective. He is awake or asleep at his discretion.
- **0200** — Arrival in Shaula system. Crew rotates to rest cycle. The protagonist's day technically begins on Day 1.

### What's off-screen

The Captain has been in port for two days, finalizing the SMC contract paperwork with TGCC's Medina office. Mooch's representative met her once. Aleyn's *Soji-Bee* is in port; he and the Captain had coffee yesterday. Kit has been in her bunk for most of the layover; she does not enjoy stations.

### Decision point

None. The Day 0 path is largely guided. The cold open hands control to the player after the Captain's first exchange.
