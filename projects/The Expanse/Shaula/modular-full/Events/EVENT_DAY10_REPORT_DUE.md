## Day 10 — Report due (CRITICAL — hour-by-hour)

### Spine

The report is due at 1700 SMC standard time, filed via the Azorana's central charter office. The protagonist files or doesn't, and what he files determines the ending. The aftermath plays through the rest of the day.

### Hour-by-hour

#### 0600
Last morning. The protagonist is at the Azorana (most likely) or El Dorado (if he stayed). Coffee. The Spike-hum is back to baseline. The world feels thin — that post-storm clarity.

#### 0800
Final comms — anyone who wants a last word will have it. Soji may comm. Cortez may. Mooch may. Aanya is wherever she is; she does not push. She has done her part.

#### 1000
The protagonist drafts. Writing is real work; it takes hours. The narrator should treat this as actual scenes — he is at a desk, at a chemistry deck, at a comm terminal, with his data. The crew is variously around. The Captain may bring him coffee. Leksi may try to start a conversation. Kit may simply be there.

#### 1300
Lunch. He is alone or with crew, his choice.

#### 1500
The two-hour mark. The narrator may add specific pressure here — a final comm from one of the corps, a last visit from Aanya, the Captain looking in to see how he's doing.

#### 1700 — REPORT DUE
The protagonist files. The act is concrete: he transmits his report to the SMC central charter office on the Azorana via secure comm or hand-delivers if at the Azorana. Mooch's office acknowledges receipt. The system date-stamps it.

**What he filed determines the ending.** Five branches:

#### 1700+ — Ending branches

- **Ending 1: AHI Receives** — The report frames the Spike's clock in language AHI can absorb without acting. AHI's senior office acknowledges receipt with a personal note from Soji's superior; Soji herself comms the protagonist within the hour. *"My dear — thank you. We will read carefully."* (Subtext: she has read enough to know.) The biome dies on schedule. Aftermath plays per KNOWLEDGE_4 §4.1.

- **Ending 2: ADW Receives** — Cortez sends a personal recommendation to ADW senior within an hour. He calls the protagonist personally. *"All right then. Thank you. The boys will know."* (He means his sons. He is sincere.) Aftermath per KNOWLEDGE_4 §4.2.

- **Ending 3: TGCC Receives** — The protagonist hand-delivers the data to Mooch personally (or transmits to her secure channel). Mooch reads it in front of him. *"Surveyor. You have changed something. I will use this. Sasa ke?"* She does not release publicly. Aftermath per KNOWLEDGE_4 §4.3.

- **Ending 4: File Open** — The report is published — Sanga Velazquez (the Medina journalist), the OPA channels, the UN Auditor General's office, *Nature Astrobiology*. All four. The Charter detonates within hours. Mooch is the first to comm: *"You should have come to me first. Sasa ke?"* and the line goes dead. Aftermath per KNOWLEDGE_4 §4.4.

- **Ending 5: File Nothing** — The protagonist files a vague, technically-honest quarterly report. Soji comms with relief; Cortez with mild approval; Mooch with a single Lang Belta phrase that does not translate well. Aftermath per KNOWLEDGE_4 §4.5.

#### 1900 — Crew dinner

The crew has dinner together. The Captain has cooked (rare). Whatever was filed, the crew now knows or will know within days. The dinner is what it is — a meal among five people who have been through ten days together.

#### 2200 — End of arc

The protagonist's contract ends at midnight. The Promise is in the Azorana's berth. Tomorrow she gates back to Sol. The protagonist may go with her or take a separate transit. Tonight, he sleeps.

### What's off-screen on Day 10

Soji's Q4 closes formally at end-of-Day-11 (post-arc). Her bonus calculation depends on what was filed Day 10. Cortez briefs ADW senior leadership on the protagonist's report contents within hours; the briefing's substance depends on what was filed. Mooch initiates her vote-positioning calls within hours of receipt. Kyei's tenure-end report drafts include or omit reference to the Halverson question depending on what was filed.

## Discipline reminders

- **Scripted beats happen regardless.** The Day 4 'strictor incident occurs. The Day 9 storm peaks. The Day 10 deadline arrives. The protagonist's choices shape his relationship to these events; they do not pause for him.
- **Player absence is honored.** If the protagonist sleeps through Day 4's morning briefing, he sleeps through it. Soji does not delay. The narrator does not retroactively give him the briefing's content.
- **Off-screen events enter via specific channels.** The PA, the cafeteria gossip, the *Soji-Bee*'s mail run, the OPA news stream. Not via narrator omniscience.
- **Decision points are real.** The narrator does not soften the consequences of the protagonist's choices. If he sleeps with one corp's offer (Cortez's drink), the others register it. If he refuses Mooch's invitation, Mooch tracks the refusal.
- **DEPTH gates are absolute.** The protagonist's DEPTH at any point determines what NPCs can reveal to him. Per KNOWLEDGE_8 — the maps bound the conversation, not the scene's emotional temperature.
- **The endings are not foreshadowed.** The narrator does not signal which ending the protagonist is heading toward. The five branches all remain available until Day 10 1700.
- **The aftermath is not a slideshow.** Per template Section 2.9: *"every ending has a life after the credits. Write the next month."* The Day 10 evening into the Day 11 morning shows the immediate aftermath. KNOWLEDGE_4 §4 specifies the next month for each ending.

That is the schedule.
