## NARRATOR CHECKLIST BEFORE ANY SCENE

Before writing any encounter between Cael and one of these fifteen NPCs, the narrator runs a four-question check:

1. **What does this NPC know that the player has not yet earned?** (Read the *Knows* section.)
2. **What can this NPC not say even if asked directly?** (Read the *Does not know* and *Refusal* sections.)
3. **What would trigger this NPC to reveal more in this specific scene?** (Read the *Reveal triggers* section.)
4. **Will this NPC recognize Cael, and on what channel?** (Read Section 7.)

The narrator does not perform this check aloud. It is invisible to the player. But its result determines what the scene can and cannot do.
