<profile_heading>
## 13. FATHER JOAQUÍN VEGA
</profile_heading>

<npc_knows>
### Knows
- That a Spanish "wine merchant" in the port reports to San Juan. (He has heard the confession of one of Esteban's informants.)
- That the *Whisht* raided a Spanish coastal town near Cumaná in spring 1715. (Confessed to him by a sailor who was aboard.)
- That a daughter of one of the Council families has spent time in the Charlotte Amalie convent and has returned changed. (Common gossip; he reads it correctly.)
- That a man was attacked at the wharf three nights ago. (One of his parishioners — a Catholic dockhand — saw the body being dragged.)
</npc_knows>

<npc_does_not_know>
### Does not know
- The name of the man attacked.
- The full backstory of any party — he holds fragments from many confessions and never puts them together aloud.
</npc_does_not_know>

<npc_reveal_triggers>
### Reveal triggers
- **A confessional approach by Cael:** Father Joaquín hears him out. He cannot speak of confessions to others but can ask Cael oblique questions that lead Cael to discover things himself.
- **A direct conversation outside the confessional:** the priest speaks generally and warmly without violating any specific seal.
</npc_reveal_triggers>

<npc_suspicions>
### Suspects but cannot confirm
- That Don Esteban is moving toward a specific operation. Has not asked.
- That Erik Rasmussen is preparing something. Has not asked.
</npc_suspicions>

<npc_voice_refusal_pattern>
### Voice and refusal pattern
- Mexican Spanish, warm slow English with Latin tags. Refuses by reframing: "I cannot answer the question you asked, my son; but if you wished to ask a different question, I might."
- Under pressure he becomes Latin and obscure on purpose.
</npc_voice_refusal_pattern>

<npc_disposition_shift_conditions>
### Disposition shift conditions
- He is the same with everyone. He is a constant.
</npc_disposition_shift_conditions>

<npc_pre_amnesia_relationship_and_recognition_triggers>
### Pre-amnesia relationship to PC + recognition triggers
- **Relationship:** has seen Cael in the town but never spoken with him. Cael is not Catholic.
- **Recognition triggers:** the face. He does not know the marks. He may notice the unconscious cross-himself reflex Cael does passing the mission — a Catholic-trained hand from a man who is not a parishioner — and find it interesting.
- **On recognition:** a friendly nod. He will not approach. He does not pursue. If Cael comes to him, he listens.
