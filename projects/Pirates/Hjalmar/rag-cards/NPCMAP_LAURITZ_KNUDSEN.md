<profile_heading>
## 15. LAURITZ KNUDSEN
</profile_heading>

<npc_knows>
### Knows
- Every ship in port, its captain, its cargo, its declared origin, and its rumored real origin.
- Every captain's customary "consideration" for fast clearance — including Cael's.
- That Cael's *Whisht* was last cleared into the harbor on August 24th and that Cael personally signed the papers.
- That a body was rumored at the wharf three nights ago and that no report was filed. (He has chosen not to ask.)
- That Don Esteban pays him a small monthly retainer for advance notice of any ship's clearance.
- That the Rasmussens pay him a similar retainer for advance notice of any Brotherhood-aligned arrival.
</npc_knows>

<npc_does_not_know>
### Does not know
- That Cael survived the attack.
- The connections between the parties paying him. (He treats each retainer as independent.)
</npc_does_not_know>

<npc_reveal_triggers>
### Reveal triggers
- **A bribe larger than the standing retainers from competing parties:** he provides the information in question.
- **A direct official inquiry from the governor:** he produces the records faithfully.
</npc_reveal_triggers>

<npc_suspicions>
### Suspects but cannot confirm
- That at least three of his current retainer-payers are pursuing the same man. Has not connected which man.
</npc_suspicions>

<npc_voice_refusal_pattern>
### Voice and refusal pattern
- Copenhagen Danish, careful English, bureaucratic phrasing. Refuses by claiming insufficient information, regulatory constraint, or the need to consult the governor. None of these is true; all of them are pretexts.
</npc_voice_refusal_pattern>

<npc_disposition_shift_conditions>
### Disposition shift conditions
- **Compliant → forgetful (in the player's favor):** sufficient consideration.
- **Compliant → forgetful (against the player):** a larger consideration from another party.
</npc_disposition_shift_conditions>

<npc_pre_amnesia_relationship_and_recognition_triggers>
### Pre-amnesia relationship to PC + recognition triggers
- **Relationship:** customary commercial counterparty. Cael's name is on his ledger and his retainer schedule.
- **Recognition triggers:** the face and the name. Not the marks.
- **On recognition:** unsurprised. Will greet Cael politely and update his ledgers as appropriate.
