## I. THE PRE-AMNESIA TIMELINE

What the named NPCs of Sankt Hjalmar remember. Surface through NPC dialogue when triggered, never as exposition.

### 1714 — Spring

- **March:** Cael's sloop *Whisht* is named and outfitted for the first time. Søren signs on as quartermaster. Hendrik refuses to retire and signs on as boatswain for three months before going ashore for good. *(Hendrik will tell this story unprompted, twice in a row, if drinking.)*
- **April:** A small Spanish coastal raid near Cumaná. *Whisht* takes a Spanish coastal trader for sugar, salt-cod, and a chest of coin. *(Don Esteban first hears the captain's name in a San Juan report. He files it.)*
- **May:** Cael meets Agnes Rasmussen at a Tuesday-market in Christianstorv. She is buying ribbon for a niece. He helps her count change because the new cashier is slow. *(Agnes remembers what she was wearing.)*

### 1714 — Summer

- **June through August:** The affair. Cael ashore most of the summer for a careening and refit. Six months of letters between Agnes and Cael moving through Karin Holm at the fish market without Karin knowing what she was passing. *(Karin remembers the recurring sealed letters; she has not yet connected them to anything important.)*
- **August:** Agnes pregnant. She tells no one for six weeks. *(Father Joaquín hears this in the confessional of a household servant named Else who suspects her mistress's morning sickness. He has held the secret since.)*

### 1714 — Autumn

- **September:** Klaus Rasmussen discovers his daughter's pregnancy. Sends her to the Convent of Our Lady of Sorrow in Charlotte Amalie on the public story of a fever. The Sankt Hjalmar packet boat carries her on the 14th. *(The packet captain, a man named Anders Pløen, remembers carrying a tearful young Council daughter. He passes through Sankt Hjalmar once a month.)*
- **October:** Cael returns from a short cruise to find Agnes gone. The household tells him she is unwell. He is given no further information. He does not press because he was never given to press where he had no right. *(Søren remembers Cael's silence on this. So does Old Hendrik, who saw Cael drinking alone at the Helm two evenings in a row for the first time.)*
- **November:** Frederik Rasmussen, Klaus's nephew, takes up the purser's berth on a Rasmussen family merchant brig out of Charlotte Amalie. The brig sails for Cádiz with a partial sugar cargo.

### 1714 — Winter

- **December:** The *Whisht* takes the Rasmussen brig in the Mona Passage. The fight is bad. Frederik Rasmussen is killed by a pistol-ball. Cael's quartermaster Søren swears it was Cael who fired. The brig's survivors are put off in a longboat. The brig's cargo is sold to a Curaçao fence through Isaac de Lima's correspondent. *(Erik Rasmussen begins, immediately, to investigate. He hires a Charlotte Amalie investigator named Pedersen who has been gathering depositions since.)*

### 1715 — Spring

- **February:** Agnes gives birth to a daughter in the Convent of Our Lady of Sorrow. The child is named Margreta by the wet-nurse who takes her — a woman named Mette Madsen, who farms a small piece of land in the hills behind Charlotte Amalie with her husband. Sister Brigitte, the abbess, tells Agnes the child is alive and where she has gone the day Agnes leaves the convent. *(Brigitte remembers Agnes's face. So does Mette Madsen, who has seen her exactly once.)*
- **March:** Agnes returns to Sankt Hjalmar. She does not speak of the convent. The Rasmussen household considers her cured of a fever. The silver crucifix at her throat is new.
- **April:** Don Esteban de Ribera receives the standing bounty notice from San Juan: one hundred pesos on the head of the *Whisht*'s captain, proof of identity required. *(Esteban has not yet been able to confirm the captain's full identity. He notes that the *Whisht*'s clearances pass through Sankt Hjalmar and begins paying Knudsen for advance notice.)*

### 1715 — Summer

- **June:** *Whisht* sails on what will be her last successful cruise. Two prizes in five weeks.
- **July 30–31:** The Spanish treasure fleet wrecks off the Florida coast in a hurricane. News reaches Sankt Hjalmar by packet on August 8. Every captain in port reorients. *(Maartens lengthens his planned cruise. de Lima rewrites three of his outstanding policies. Knudsen begins receiving bribes from new directions. Don Esteban gets fresh urgency from San Juan.)*
- **August 24:** *Whisht* limps back to Sankt Hjalmar undermanned and damaged after a brush with a Spanish patrol vessel south of Mona. Cael personally signs her papers into the harbor with Knudsen. *(Knudsen remembers Cael's face was bruised. He did not ask.)*
- **August 26:** Cael pays off most of his crew. Keeps only Søren and two others on retaining wage.
- **Late August:** Cael drinks at the Helm three evenings in a row. Sees Agnes once in the market. She does not see him. Or she does and pretends. *(Hendrik remembers Cael's silence those evenings. Cael did not speak to anyone, including Hendrik.)*
- **September 9 (Friday):** Søren accepts his fifth monthly payment from Erik Rasmussen via an intermediary. The payment includes a specific question — *what is the captain's drinking schedule this week.*

### 1715 — Three nights before the campaign opens

- **September 12 (Friday) evening:** Cael drinks alone at the Helm. He leaves at approximately 11:30 p.m.
- **September 12 (Friday) — 11:45 p.m.:** Three men hired by Erik are waiting in the alley behind the Helm. Stickjaw, Jens Kruse (a hired Charlotte Amalie dockhand), and a third man — Mads Holst, who Cael kills with a knife to the femoral artery in the first sixty seconds of the fight.
- **September 12 (Friday) — midnight:** Cael is struck on the back of the head with a belaying pin. Dragged toward the wharf. Stickjaw, panicking after recognizing Cael during the fight, rolls the body off the wharf without weighing it. He and Jens Kruse leave separately. Mads Holst's body is hauled into the harbor by Stickjaw before they leave; it has not yet surfaced. *(Karin Holm sees her brother come home with blood on his shirt and asks no questions.)*
- **September 13 (Saturday) — dawn:** Petter Halvorsen, working the leeward beach with his small boat, finds Cael in the shallows. Carries him to Eliza Free in the highlands.
- **September 13 (Saturday) through September 14 (Sunday):** Cael unconscious or feverish in Eliza Free's cottage. Eliza recognizes the thigh-marks. She tells no one, not even Madre Joana.
- **September 14 (Sunday) — midnight:** Jens Kruse boards a Charlotte Amalie packet under a false name. He is gone before anyone in Sankt Hjalmar knows the job did not go as Erik commissioned.
- **September 15 (Monday) — dawn:** Cael wakes knowing nothing. Eliza walks him to the edge of town on the path down from the leeward beach. She does not give him her name. She does not return after he turns to look.

**The campaign opens at this point.**
