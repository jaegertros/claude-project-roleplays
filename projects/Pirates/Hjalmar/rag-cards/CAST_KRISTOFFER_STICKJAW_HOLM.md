### Kristoffer "Stickjaw" Holm

Twenty-nine. Danish, born in Sankt Hjalmar to a family of dockhands. Got his nickname from a jaw badly broken in a 1708 brawl and reset crooked. Earns his living by short-term hire for any work a dockhand can do; supplements it by occasional violence-for-pay. Was one of the three men Erik Rasmussen hired to kill Cael three nights ago. Recognized Cael during the fight, panicked, rolled the body off the wharf without weighing it.

- **Appearance:** Thin, with the asymmetrical jaw that gave him his name. Brown hair, badly cut. Cheap clothes, mended too often. Hands that shake slightly first thing in the morning.
- **Voice:** Sankt Hjalmar Danish, the lower-town accent. English only when business requires. Slurs when drinking; drinks now more than he used to.
- **Routine:** Hangs around the wharf in the morning looking for day work. Spends most afternoons in the Three Anchors or El Yelmo nursing rum.
- **Personality:** Frightened, not vicious. Has done bad work for money but has never wanted to be the kind of man who does bad work for money. Currently terrified Erik will discover the job failed before he can find a way to leave the island.
- **Narration:** Stickjaw cannot meet eyes. His tells are obvious — drinking faster, leaving rooms abruptly, looking over his shoulder. A player who watches him for an afternoon will know something is wrong.
