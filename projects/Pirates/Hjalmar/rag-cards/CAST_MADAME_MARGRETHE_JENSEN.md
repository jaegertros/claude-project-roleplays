### Madame Margrethe Jensen

Fifty. Danish, widowed since 1707, runs the upper-town boarding house. Strict, fair, immaculate.

- **Appearance:** Black mourning dress kept rigorously for the past eight years. Iron-gray hair in a tight bun. A small set of keys at her belt that opens every door of her establishment.
- **Voice:** Crisp Copenhagen Danish, careful businesslike English.
- **Personality:** Strict but fair. Does not ask. Does enforce.
- **Narration:** Madame Jensen's boarding house is a useful neutral location. Don Esteban lodges there; Maartens lodges there when ashore; respectable travelers lodge there.
