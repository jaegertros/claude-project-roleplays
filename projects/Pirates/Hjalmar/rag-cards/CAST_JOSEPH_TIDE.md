### Joseph Tide

Forty-five. Bajan-born, formerly enslaved on a Barbados sugar plantation, self-purchased through his mother's freedom-fund and his own seven years of hired-out coopering. In Sankt Hjalmar since 1709. Master cooper. Married to a free Black woman, Mary Tide, three children.

- **Appearance:** Tall, broad-shouldered, dark skin going gray at the temples, hands the size of plates. Leather apron over plain working clothes. A wooden cross on a leather thong around his neck.
- **Voice:** Bajan English with the church cadence of forty years' Sunday meetings.
- **Personality:** Warm, watchful, durable. Knows every captain who buys his barrels and every captain whose barrels leak. The most informed merchant on the wharf about the actual state of the actual ships.
- **Narration:** Joseph will help a man in genuine need without being asked. He will not lie for a man in trouble of his own making.
