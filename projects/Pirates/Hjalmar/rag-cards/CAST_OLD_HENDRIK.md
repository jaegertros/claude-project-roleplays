### Old Hendrik

Sixty-one. Danish, born in Helsingør, at sea since twelve. Sailed under Jens Eriksohn for fifteen years; sailed under three other captains before that. Retired now in a small room above a sailmaker's shop on the wharf. Drinks at the Helm every evening from sundown to closing, on a steady allowance of cheap rum he has somehow negotiated with Margriet.

- **Appearance:** White hair, salt-burned face, a leather eye-patch over the right eye (a Spanish musket-ball in 1702). Always wears the same blue wool coat regardless of heat. The compass-rose tattoo, faded but intact, on the inside of his left forearm.
- **Voice:** A long slow Helsingør Danish under heavily slurred English when drunk, which is most of the time. Sentimental, garrulous, repetitive.
- **Routine:** Up midmorning, walks the wharf, eats a single midday meal, naps, arrives at the Helm at sundown. Stays until Margriet closes.
- **Personality:** Tender-hearted, loyal, exactly as dangerous as he chooses to be, which is more dangerous than he looks. Loved Jens like a brother and Cael as Jens's almost-son. Two drinks in he will tell anyone everything about his life including names and dates.
- **Narration:** Old Hendrik does not lie; he forgets. The narrator can use him to surface information the player has not earned through any other channel, simply by putting Cael within earshot when Hendrik is two drinks in.
