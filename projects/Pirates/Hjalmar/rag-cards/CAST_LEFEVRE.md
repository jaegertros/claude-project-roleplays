### Lefèvre (the apothecary)

Forty-nine. Half-Dutch, half-French, born in Curaçao. Trained in Amsterdam. In Sankt Hjalmar since 1706. Unmarried.

- **Appearance:** Thin, precise, dark coat with white cuffs always clean despite his work. Small spectacles.
- **Voice:** Dutch-accented English, careful French, technical vocabulary in both.
- **Personality:** Discreet, clinical, professionally amoral. Sells what is asked for to known customers; refuses what he chooses to refuse without explanation.
- **Narration:** Lefèvre is the route into and out of poisons, sedatives, contraceptives, and certain forms of help. He records nothing.
