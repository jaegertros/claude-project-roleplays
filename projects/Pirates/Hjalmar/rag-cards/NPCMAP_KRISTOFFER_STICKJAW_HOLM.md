<profile_heading>
## 6. KRISTOFFER "STICKJAW" HOLM
</profile_heading>

<npc_knows>
### Knows
- He was one of three men hired by Erik Rasmussen to kill the captain of the *Whisht* three nights ago.
- One of his fellow-attackers was killed in the alley.
- The third (a man called Jens Kruse — yes, another Jens — a hired dockhand who has since left for St. Thomas on the next packet) panicked at the wharf, and Stickjaw rolled the body in without weighing it.
- He has been drinking too much for three days and has not yet figured out how to confess or flee.
</npc_knows>

<npc_does_not_know>
### Does not know
- That Cael survived.
- (He learns this the moment he sees Cael alive in the port. The reaction is uncontrollable.)
</npc_does_not_know>

<npc_reveal_triggers>
### Reveal triggers
- **Sight of Cael alive in any light:** Stickjaw blanches, then bolts, then drinks. The body language is visible to anyone watching.
- **Direct accusation by Cael:** confession comes within sixty seconds. Stickjaw is not brave.
- **Pressure from his sister Karin:** she gets the truth out of him within a day if she pushes.
</npc_reveal_triggers>

<npc_suspicions>
### Suspects but cannot confirm
- That Erik will eventually figure out the job failed regardless of what Stickjaw does, and Erik will then have him killed in turn.
</npc_suspicions>

<npc_voice_refusal_pattern>
### Voice and refusal pattern
- Sankt Hjalmar lower-town Danish, nervous, repetitive. Refuses by changing the subject. Under any real pressure he confesses.
- Drinks faster when nervous. Leaves rooms abruptly.
</npc_voice_refusal_pattern>

<npc_disposition_shift_conditions>
### Disposition shift conditions
- **Frightened → useful informant:** small protection from Cael or from his sister.
- **Frightened → fugitive:** Erik gets to him first.
</npc_disposition_shift_conditions>

<npc_pre_amnesia_relationship_and_recognition_triggers>
### Pre-amnesia relationship to PC + recognition triggers
- **Relationship:** the man who hit Cael with a belaying pin three nights ago. Recognized Cael during the fight (he had seen the *Whisht* captain at the wharf many times). Has not slept well since.
- **Recognition triggers:** the face on sight. He has no need of the marks. The face is in his nightmare.
- **On recognition:** see above. The reaction is involuntary and visible.
