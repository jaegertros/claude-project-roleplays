## III. TRIGGER EVENTS

Events that fire only when a specific player action meets a specific narrator-side condition. The narrator does not pre-announce these; they activate when the trigger fires.

- **Cael walks past the Mahamad House on a Saturday morning while the service is in progress.** A small commotion as he passes — an old Sephardic merchant, Aaron de Castro, recognizes him from a 1714 transaction and turns to point him out to a fellow worshipper. The pointing is visible; the recognition is brief. de Castro will not speak in the street; if Cael returns at another time, de Castro will engage carefully.
- **Cael wears his coat with the sleeves rolled in any tavern.** If Old Hendrik is present and has had two drinks, see his reveal trigger in NPC_Maps. The most likely tavern-wide accident in the campaign.
- **Cael says "Whisht" aloud in Søren's chandlery.** Søren cannot pretend he did not hear it. The decision he has been postponing arrives.
- **Cael says "Whisht" aloud in any tavern.** Anyone who has sailed in the Brotherhood the past two years pauses. Recognition cascades depending on who is in the room — see NPC_Maps for each present NPC.
- **Cael speaks Cherokee or Danish aloud in Eliza Free's hearing.** She responds in the same language. The narrator should treat this as a Body-knows moment that opens a Player-knows door if Cael chooses to follow.
- **Cael enters the Helm in the evening and Hendrik is present.** Roll the recognition channels in order — first sight (the eyebrow scar visible across the room), then voice (one word from Cael), then the tattoo if Cael's sleeves are rolled. Any one is sufficient. Hendrik will name him aloud.
- **Cael enters Lefèvre's apothecary and asks for any treatment.** Lefèvre's professional discretion: he will treat Cael's bruises and the fading welt on the back of his head without comment. But Lefèvre will also note the patient's face for future reference, and if Don Esteban asks, Lefèvre will mention the visit.
- **Cael walks the upper-town streets in daylight wearing the coat he washed up in.** The garrison sees him. If Tomás O'Brien is on duty, see his reveal triggers in NPC_Maps.
- **Cael shows the folded paper to any educated NPC.** The handwriting will be recognized as Cael's own by anyone who has seen him sign a clearance, a manifest, or a bill of lading in the past two years. That list includes Knudsen, de Lima, and Søren.
- **Cael is bathed, shaved, and properly dressed for the first time since waking.** He becomes visibly the man he used to be to anyone who knew him. The recognition rate doubles for the rest of the day.
