### Big Anna

Thirty-eight. Saint-Domingue-born, formerly enslaved on a French sugar plantation, escaped 1701 with a knife wound that nearly killed her. In Sankt Hjalmar since 1705. Master smith. Unmarried by choice.

- **Appearance:** Tall, heavily muscled, dark skin marked by old burns from her trade and one long pale scar across her left forearm from the escape. Working leathers, sleeves rolled, hair tied back under a kerchief.
- **Voice:** Haitian French-creole under careful English. Few words.
- **Personality:** Strong, silent, observant, capable of sudden violence and capable of equally sudden kindness. The only person in the town who has openly broken a knife across her knee in front of a customer (a sailor in 1712, after he made a remark; he left without his knife and without his teeth).
- **Narration:** Anna does not warn. She acts. The narrator writes her silences and her hands.
