### Captain Pieter Maartens

Forty-four. Dutch, born in Rotterdam, at sea since fourteen. Currently captain of the brigantine *De Vrije Wind* (*The Free Wind*), in port this season for a careening and a refit. Has known Cael since 1714 by the Brotherhood network; sailed briefly under Jens Eriksohn in his youth. Carries the crew tattoo, intact.

- **Appearance:** Stocky, red-blond beard going gray at the chin, blue eyes, a livid burn-scar across the back of his right hand. Practical sea clothes ashore — no fashionable display.
- **Voice:** Heavy Dutch under English, businesslike, blunt. Switches to Dutch with his own crew, to Spanish with fences, to French with smugglers.
- **Routine:** Aboard *De Vrije Wind* most days, supervising the careenage work. Drinks at the Three Anchors most evenings. Has lodgings at Jensen's when his ship is not habitable.
- **Personality:** Honest in the specific way pirates are honest — keeps his word inside the trade, has no obligation to anyone outside it. Liked Cael, respected Jens, dislikes Erik Rasmussen on principle (he distrusts men who do not work with their hands).
- **Narration:** Maartens treats Cael as a colleague the moment he sees him. He does not realize anything is wrong unless Cael explicitly tells him.
