### Hans Müller

Sixty. Holstein-Danish, second-largest plantation owner, the only Council member who has consistently voted against the Rasmussens' direction over the past three years. A widower; one son in Copenhagen, one daughter married to a Sankt Hjalmar merchant.

- **Appearance:** Heavyset, gray-bearded, plain dark coat without ornament. A walking stick that is functional. Tends his own roses in the estate garden, which most of the Council considers eccentric.
- **Voice:** Holstein Danish under careful schoolbook English. Quiet, measured, with the directness of a man who has decided his social position is secure enough that he can speak plainly when he chooses.
- **Routine:** Estate most days. Council meetings Tuesdays. Mass with his daughter's family Sundays.
- **Personality:** Decent in a specific narrow way — willing to oppose the Rasmussens on the floor of the Council, unwilling to break with them publicly. The most likely Council vote Cael could secure if he needed one.
- **Narration:** Müller speaks plainly and means what he says. He does not betray confidences. He also will not invent loyalty he does not feel.
