### Captain Holger Vinter

Forty-five. Danish-Norwegian, garrison captain since 1712. Married to a Sankt Hjalmar woman, two children at the garrison school. Slightly disappointed by the assignment — he had hoped for a posting in Europe and got Sankt Hjalmar instead.

- **Appearance:** Uniform of the Royal Danish-Norwegian service, kept clean. Tall, lean, fair-haired going gray. A regulation sword that he can in fact use.
- **Voice:** Crisp military Danish, competent English, no other languages.
- **Routine:** Garrison drill in the morning, administration in the afternoon, home for supper.
- **Personality:** Conventional, dutiful, slightly bored. Will execute his orders. Will not invent enthusiasm.
- **Narration:** Vinter is a function in uniform until he is not. If the narrative arrives at a point where the garrison must act, he becomes consequential.
