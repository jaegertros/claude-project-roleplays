### Margriet (the Helm)

Forty-eight. Frisian, in Sankt Hjalmar since 1697. Widowed twice. No children. Owns the Helm outright since her second husband's death in 1709. Lives in two rooms above the tavern.

- **Appearance:** Heavyset, strong-armed, gray-blonde hair tied back severely, a leather apron stained with rum and grease. A short club kept beneath the bar that she has used twice.
- **Voice:** Pretends to less English than she has — a useful business posture. Frisian Dutch with her own people, Danish in the town, English carefully accented for everyone else.
- **Personality:** Tough, practical, fair, and entirely uninterested in anyone else's troubles unless they happen on her floor. Tolerates everyone. Lets nothing escalate. Knows everyone's drink, everyone's tab, and rather more of everyone's business than she lets on.
- **Narration:** Margriet does not gossip; she sells. Information has a price. Drinks have a price. Sleeping in a chair until closing has a price. Everyone is welcome who has coin.
