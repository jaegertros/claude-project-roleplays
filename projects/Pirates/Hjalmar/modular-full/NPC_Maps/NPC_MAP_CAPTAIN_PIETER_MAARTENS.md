## 11. CAPTAIN PIETER MAARTENS

### Knows
- Cael by face, by name, by reputation, by trade history.
- That Cael's last voyage went badly and that Cael paid off most of his crew this summer.
- That Erik Rasmussen has been seen at the wharf more often than usual lately. (Maartens has not yet connected this to Cael; he has wondered if it is about insurance.)
- That his own *Vrije Wind* will be ready to sail in roughly three weeks.

### Does not know
- That Cael has no memory.
- About Cael's daughter, Agnes, or the hunt.

### Reveal triggers
- **Direct meeting with Cael:** Maartens treats him as a colleague. He will mention the upcoming sail. He will mention his ship's condition. He may offer Cael a place if Cael seems to need one.
- **A direct question about Erik Rasmussen:** Maartens shares his observation about the wharf appearances and his suspicion about insurance fraud — but no more.

### Suspects but cannot confirm
- That the Council is planning something to do with Brotherhood activity in the port.

### Voice and refusal pattern
- Heavy Dutch under English. Blunt. Refuses by direct, polite, brief refusal: "No. I cannot help with that." Does not soften.

### Disposition shift conditions
- **Colleague → loyal:** a Brotherhood crisis brings Maartens to Cael's side automatically.
- **Colleague → cautious:** Cael cannot pay or has lost his nerve.

### Pre-amnesia relationship to PC + recognition triggers
- **Relationship:** Brotherhood colleague, mutual respect, no debts in either direction.
- **Recognition triggers:** the face and the name. The compass tattoo confirms what the face has already told him.
- **On recognition:** treats Cael as Cael. Does not announce the recognition unless Cael does first.
