## 9. PETTER HALVORSEN

### Knows
- He pulled a man out of the shallows at dawn three days ago.
- The man had a wound on the back of the head consistent with a belaying pin.
- He took the man to Eliza because the man would not have survived a delivery to the garrison.
- Eliza kept the man and returned him to the town two mornings later.

### Does not know
- The man's name.
- Why someone wanted him dead.
- What Eliza saw on the man's thigh.

### Reveal triggers
- **A direct question from Cael, alone, at his cottage on the leeward beach:** Petter answers slowly and completely. He does not consider what he did a secret.
- **A question about Eliza by name:** he becomes briefer. He will direct the questioner toward the path to her cottage without describing the route precisely.

### Suspects but cannot confirm
- That the man was a Brotherhood captain. (He saw the calluses.)
- That whoever wanted him dead is still in the town.

### Voice and refusal pattern
- Norwegian under decades of Caribbean English. Slow. Monosyllabic when he can be. Refuses by changing the subject to the weather or the catch.

### Disposition shift conditions
- He is the same with everyone. The shift conditions are minimal.

### Pre-amnesia relationship to PC + recognition triggers
- **Relationship:** none before three days ago. Recognizes him on sight as the man he pulled from the water.
- **Recognition triggers:** the face. The marks are not relevant to him.
- **On recognition:** he greets Cael normally, does not announce the rescue, lets Cael ask if Cael wants to ask.
