## II. THE SCHEDULED CALENDAR

Events that will happen at the specified time regardless of player action. The narrator does not need to introduce them visibly until the date arrives.

### Week 1

| Day | Event |
|---|---|
| Day 1 (Monday) | Campaign opens. Cael at the edge of town at mid-morning. |
| Day 1 (Monday) afternoon | The brigantine *Sankt Anna* (Danish merchant, 80 tons) is clearing Customs to sail Wednesday for Charlotte Amalie. Knudsen handling. |
| Day 2 (Tuesday) | Market in Christianstorv. Council meets at Government House at 2 p.m. (Klaus, Erik, Hans Müller, four other senior Council members present.) |
| Day 2 (Tuesday) afternoon | Hans Müller will publicly press Klaus on the proposed expansion of the slave market — a vote he has been losing for months. Erik will deflect. |
| Day 3 (Wednesday) evening | Methodist meeting at Ezekiel Boatswain's. |
| Day 3 (Wednesday) — late | *Sankt Anna* sails for Charlotte Amalie carrying mail. Karin Holm has a letter aboard from her brother Stickjaw to a cousin in Charlotte Amalie. The letter is incoherent and asks for nothing in particular; she included it because he asked. |
| Day 4 (Thursday) afternoon | A Spanish patrol vessel — the schooner *Concepción de Santa Isabel* out of San Juan — anchors briefly in the harbor mouth, sends a longboat ashore, and departs after two hours. The lieutenant aboard reports to Don Esteban. *(Routine. Esteban learns nothing actionable. But the patrol's presence is noted by every Brotherhood captain in port, including Maartens.)* |
| Day 5 (Friday) morning | A letter from the Convent of Our Lady of Sorrow arrives at the Rasmussen estate on the morning packet from Charlotte Amalie. The letter is addressed to Klaus and asks discreetly about the continuing fees for the child Margreta. Klaus has not yet read it; it is on his desk. *(Erik will read it before Klaus does if Klaus does not return to his study by midday Saturday.)* |
| Day 5 (Friday) evening | Sephardic Sabbath at the Mahamad House. de Lima will be there. |
| Day 7 (Sunday) morning | Christianskirke service. Pastor Bagger preaches. Klaus and Erik attend; Agnes attends with them. *(If Cael is in the upper town that morning, he may see her at a distance walking to or from the church. She will see him at the same distance.)* |

### Week 2

| Day | Event |
|---|---|
| Day 8 (Monday) | Full moon (Julian calendar). Tides larger than the rest of the week. |
| Day 9 (Tuesday) | Market and Council meeting. Erik will quietly raise — for the first time, in private after the meeting — the topic of "a returned man on the wharf" to one trusted Council ally. *(This is the moment the second-phase hunt begins to organize. The conversation is overheard by a household servant, Else, who reports to Klaus through her own channels.)* |
| Day 10 (Wednesday) | **Convergence window opens.** See Section IV. |
| Day 11 (Thursday) afternoon | Maartens's *De Vrije Wind* completes her careening. She begins reloading provisions. Three weeks to sail unless something delays. |
| Day 12 (Friday) | Another packet from Charlotte Amalie arrives. Possible second letter from the convent or from the Madsen family about the child's care. |
| Day 13 (Saturday) afternoon | Hans Müller hosts a small dinner at his town residence. Several "middling" Council families attend. Klaus does not. *(If Cael has surfaced as a political question, this dinner is where Müller will ask his guests what they have heard.)* |
| Day 14 (Sunday) | **Convergence window closes.** By end of day at least two ignition paths are active. |

### Week 3 and beyond

- **Day 16–17:** A hurricane warning will reach the port if the long swell from the southeast continues. The narrator does not need to commit to a hurricane *hitting*; the threat is enough to reshape every plan in the port.
- **Day 18 (Thursday):** Maartens's *Vrije Wind* finishes provisioning. Could sail Day 21 if weather permits.
- **Day 21 (Sunday):** Earliest possible date for *Vrije Wind* to sail. Maartens may offer Cael a berth depending on what has happened by this point.
- **Day 22 (Monday):** End of month. End-of-month packet to Curaçao departs. Last reliable mail before the height of hurricane season.
- **Day 28 (Sunday):** Likely week of Henry Jennings's raid on the Spanish salvage camp at the Florida wreck site. *(This is roughly the date the rumor of the raid reaches Sankt Hjalmar — about a week after the raid itself. By the time the rumor arrives, Brotherhood captains who can refit fast are already gone.)*
