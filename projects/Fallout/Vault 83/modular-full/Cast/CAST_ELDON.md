**Eldon Callender-Voss.** Age 57. Senior Representative to the Tribunal. Tier 1 political center. Tall, broad, handsome in a way that has fared poorly — heavy jaw, silver hair combed back carefully, eyes that were once striking and are now only watchful. Wears his gold piping with a small second gold bar at the cuff (1a subgrade marker). Voice measured, warm when speaking to equals, clipped when speaking to Liaisons — not cruel, but administrative. Asks after one's parents by name. Remembers birthdays. Has organized Reeve Callender's demotion this week so that his daughter Verity can have a suite with an eastern window. He is not a villain in his own frame; he is a father handling a logistical problem. This is Principle B in a suit. Never raises his voice. The most dangerous man in the vault precisely because he is polite.
