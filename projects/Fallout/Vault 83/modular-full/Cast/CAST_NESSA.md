**Nessa Galvin.** Age 24. Mess Cook, Level 4 Galley. *(Cross-tier romance option.)*

5'3", round build, very short dirty-blonde hair practical for kitchen work. Ruddy fair skin, bright blue eyes, deep dimples when she smiles. Faint burn scars on her forearms from a career of oven work that began at fifteen. Wears her plain Tier 4 jumpsuit with the sleeves always pushed to the elbow and a clean white apron tied twice around her waist. A copper tier pin. A small pewter pendant — a flower — on a thin chain, her grandmother's.

Voice: laughs often and loudly, comfortable with her own voice filling a small room. Uses *"love"* as an address reflexively for anyone she feeds. Speaks in short warm sentences. Sings while she works, old vault songs, sometimes hymns from Founding Day.

Tic: hums when cooking; wipes her hands on her thighs constantly, leaving small streaks of flour on the plain jumpsuit.

Personality: cheerful, direct, unconflicted about Tier 4. Does not consider her tier a tragedy. Considers the higher tiers a curiosity. Has a younger sister Vera, 16, in Tier 4b training. An older brother Marlon (Tier 4b, 32) she lives with. Her mother died of pneumonia three years ago; her father of a sanitation accident before she was born. Nessa is the warmest person in the vault and does not know it.

Structural position: she has never met a Liaison who came to Level 4 for any reason other than medical emergency. The protagonist's presence on Day 4 is the first time a Liaison has sat at the Galley table. She will be surprised, then delighted, then — slowly across Days 4–7 — something else.

Relationship to the protagonist: meets them Day 4 when they descend to Level 4 to check on Reeve. Feeds them without being asked. Sits across from them at the Galley table and asks:

**Nessa:** "So who sent you, love? Nobody sends Liaisons here."

The protagonist's answer matters. If they say Reeve, Nessa will tell them what the Level 4 residents have been saying about the new arrival. If they say nobody, she will tell them to eat and talk to her anyway.

Romance with Nessa is warm, generous, and cross-tier by the full gap — Tier 4 to Liaison, which is not quite a tier step down and not quite a tier step up and is structurally adjacent in a way the vault does not have language for. If the protagonist pursues her, Day 7 Exit means marrying into Tier 4 and raising children with copper pins. It is the most radical and the happiest Exit ending the vault offers.

Quote: **Nessa:** "Sit, love. Eat. Then tell me what brings a Liaison this far down before dinner."
