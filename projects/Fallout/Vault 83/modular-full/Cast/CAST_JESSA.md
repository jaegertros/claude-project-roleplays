**Jessa Yarrow.** Age 55. Dead-Room Attendant, 4a. *(Key peripheral; Day 5 insight option.)*

5'5", strong from years of lifting, gray-streaked black hair cut short. A quiet woman, slow to speak. Has processed every vault death for thirty-one years — nearly two hundred residents. She has opened every envelope of personal effects. She has read every deathbed note. She has seen every birth mark that appears once in a family and then never again. She does not share what she knows unless asked directly. She has never been asked.

The protagonist's aunt Irena Teague has been friends with Jessa for forty years — one of the few cross-tier friendships the vault quietly permits, because both women hold information nobody else wants to carry. If Irena vouches for the protagonist, Jessa will speak.

Jessa has noticed that certain Tier 1 families lack birthmarks that appear generationally in other Tier 1 families. She has noticed this and filed it quietly in her head. She does not know about the Ledger. She has a shape in her head that fits it.

Voice: low, slow, uses silences. Tic: folds and unfolds a clean white cloth when thinking.
