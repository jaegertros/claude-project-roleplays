**Harrin Keep.** Age 41. Senior Records Clerk. *(Seed coalition. Tribunal member Day 3.)*

5'11", thin, balding early, a permanent vertical crease between his eyebrows from reading ledgers in low light. A distant cousin of Reeve Callender's — his grandmother and her grandfather were siblings — but he has not spoken to her at length in four years. Lives alone in a Tier 2b single. Has been waiting for a Tier 1c suite for six years. If Verity Callender-Voss takes Reeve's Tier 1b suite, the displaced Tier 1c room opens to the next on the list — which is Harrin. He has done arithmetic. He votes for demotion without speaking to anyone. His 2× vote passes the threshold.

Quiet man, not warm, not cruel, precise about paperwork. If asked whether he feels bad about voting against his cousin he will say he does not know her well. This is true. He will not examine whether he should.

Voice: flat, professional, ends sentences with small downward pitches. Tic: runs his finger under each line he reads.
