The only Tier 1 resident who descends to Level 4 regularly, and only with a Liaison escort. Treats everyone — genuinely does — but believes Level 4 residents "present differently" and is wrong about it in a soft unexamined way. Married to a Tier 1b nurse. Two adult children, one placed 1b and one 1c. No curiosity about the vault's foundations. Competent, kind, inadequate.
