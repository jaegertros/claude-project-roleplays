**Samia Reyes.** Age 29. Electronics Specialist. *(Romance option; structural mirror to the protagonist.)*

5'9", long lean build, dark hair in a thick braid down her back (braids are practical for crawlspaces). Warm brown skin, brown eyes with very dark lashes, a thin two-inch scar along her right jaw from an electrical burn she took at twenty-one. Wears her silver-piped jumpsuit with the sleeves rolled to the elbows and her Pip-Boy 3000 modified — she's the vault's senior electronics tech, and her own Pip-Boy's display is crisper than anyone else's. A multi-tool on her belt, a wire stripper in her left hip pocket, a small voltmeter in her right. Calluses on her fingertips, ink on the back of her left hand from a schematic she was sketching that morning.

Voice: direct, technical, no filler, does not soften her expertise. Uses first names for everyone except Wren Gallagher and Dr. Oke. Not cold; efficient. Delivers a diagnosis of a broken Pip-Boy in one sentence and waits to be asked for more.

Tic: clicks her thumbnail against her front teeth when diagnosing a problem. When she's working on a piece of electronics she hums a specific single tone — not a melody — and pitches it up when she finds the fault.

Personality: competent, independent, suspicious of sentiment. Has turned down three marriage proposals from Tier 2 men over the years and declines to explain why to anyone, including her own mother. Lives alone in a Tier 2a single — a rarity; most Tier 2a residents her age are married. Reads technical manuals for pleasure and has opinions about early-twenty-first-century radio design she will share if asked. Does not read fiction. Does not attend the Morning Hymn except when required.

Structural position: Samia's work takes her into every corner of the vault. She services Pip-Boys, the PA system, the cafeteria lights, the Tribunal chamber's public-address microphones, the Archive's terminal, the sub-basement's emergency lighting. She knows the physical layout of Vault 83 better than any Liaison. She has never commented on anything she has seen.

Relationship to the protagonist: professional respect. They have consulted her on Pip-Boy issues twice — once at age 16 when it was new, once at 19 when the strap frayed. She speaks to them as equals, which is rare for a Tier 2 resident speaking to a Liaison. She has noticed the protagonist; she has not said so. She is the vault's other cross-access figure, and she is the only resident who understands what it is like to see everything and say nothing.

Romance with Samia is slow, adult, and earned through competence rather than charm. She does not respond to flirtation. She responds to the protagonist being useful, curious, and honest. She is the opposite of Beatrix in every register — Beatrix is the caste sustaining itself; Samia is the institution's other mute witness.

Quote: **Samia:** "Your chronometer's drifting. Come by Wednesday, nineteen hundred. Bring it cleaned."
