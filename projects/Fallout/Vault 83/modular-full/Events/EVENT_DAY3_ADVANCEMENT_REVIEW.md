## DAY 3 — WEDNESDAY — THE ADVANCEMENT REVIEW

The day. Committed hour-by-hour.

**06:30** — Wake. The vault knows. The corridor kitchen is quieter than usual. Mae Teague does not comment.

**Ezra:** "Easy day for you. Delivery to Reeve at seven. Envelope to Mrs. Hoad at eleven-thirty. Tribunal stationing at fourteen-hundred. Escort at seventeen-forty-five."

The protagonist's assignments are given deliberately early so they can prepare.

**07:00** — **Deliver petition notice to Reeve Callender (1b-09).** She answers the door in a dressing gown, half-shaven, dark hair unpinned at her shoulders. She reads the notice silently. Sits. Protocol requires the protagonist wait in the doorway for a return acknowledgment. The pause is long. She finally stands, signs the receipt with a fountain pen from her desk, hands it back, and says:

**Reeve:** "Thank you for doing your job, Mr. Teague."

Flat. Not angry. Not accusing. Done. The protagonist leaves. The door closes.

**07:45** — Morning Hymn. Reeve is present in the Tier 1b row. She does not sing. No one looks at her directly.

**08:00** — Morning Address. Wren delivers a brief general address. Does not mention the Tribunal.

**09:00–11:00** — Small runs. Delivery to Dr. Oke. A document to the Archive.

**11:30 — Carry sealed envelope from Eldon's office to Mira Hoad (2a-07).** Heavy envelope — a folded sheet and a small item. Mira takes it, does not open in front of the protagonist.

**Mira:** "Thank you, dear."

Door closes.

*The envelope contains: a written commitment from Eldon that Mira's apartment 2a-07 will not be reassigned after her mother Orla's death if Reese remains Tier 3. The small item is a pressed flower — symbolic. Eldon is paying for Mira's vote in advance, in the currency she values. Mira does not frame it to herself as being paid. She will frame her vote as a judgment about Reeve.*

**12:00** — Lunch. Reeve eats alone in the Tier 1 Dining Hall. Miriam Callender sits at the other end of the hall and does not approach.

**13:45** — The protagonist reports to the corridor outside the Tribunal Chamber. Sits on the bench. Waits.

**14:00–16:00 — THE HEARING.** The protagonist does not enter. Tribunal composition:

| Member | Tier | Weight | Vote | Motive |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Wren Gallagher | 1a | 3× | Abstain (ceremonial) | — |
| Eldon Callender-Voss | 1a | 3× | **YES** | Housing for daughter Verity |
| Felix Brunell | 1b | 3× | Abstain | Moral cowardice (owed Reeve favors) |
| Harrin Keep | 2b | 2× | **YES** | Next in line for Tier 1c suite displaced by Verity |
| Mira Hoad | 2a | 2× | **YES** | Keep son Reese in Tier 3 / keep apartment |
| Tess Oloyede | 3a | 1× | **YES** | Expanded hydroponics plot |
| Marta Thale | 4b | 1× | **NO** | Class solidarity — Tier 4 votes against demotion on principle |

Weighted tally: **YES 8 / NO 1 / Abstain 6.** Threshold: 6. Demotion passes.

The protagonist observes body language between breaks. Harrin is tense. Mira is carefully neutral. Tess reads a report to avoid eye contact. Eldon is relaxed — he is in his element. Felix comes out once with a hand pressed to his face.

Reeve is not present. The accused does not attend their own hearing. She is in 1b-09 packing.

**16:30** — The Tribunal concludes. Members exit. Wren carries the decision to the Administrator's office for PA transmission.

**17:30 — THE CALL.**

**PA:** "By decision of the Tribunal, convened this day: Dr. Reeve Callender, General Education, Tier 1b, is placed upon demotion. Her new assignment: Tier 4, sanitation apprenticeship. Her new residence: Level 4, Apartment 4b-11. Effective immediately. This is Vault-Tec."

The tone is pleasant. The words are operational.

**17:45 — ESCORT.** The protagonist reports to 1b-09. Reeve opens the door in her work jumpsuit (still Tier 1b; her Tier 4 plain jumpsuit and copper pin will be issued at 4b-11). She has one small leather suitcase — her father's, 2076-issue. The rest of her belongings will be sent down by Tier 1 maintenance staff over the next two days.

She does not speak during the elevator descent. The protagonist can feel the temperature rise as they pass through Level 3 and into Level 4 — the warmer air from the geothermal returns.

The elevator opens on Level 4. Empty corridor. Nessa Galvin is in the Galley preparing dinner — it is 17:55, she is running late — and hears footsteps but does not come out.

They reach 4b-11. The protagonist opens the door with the Tier 4 keycard Ezra issued that morning. Small gray room. Bed, dresser, desk, chair, overhead fluorescent. Clean. The previous occupant's dried lavender sachet in the top dresser drawer.

Reeve steps in. Sets her suitcase on the bed. Does not unpack.

She turns to the protagonist.

**Reeve:** "Did you know this was coming?"

The response tree (player options, then Reeve's reply to each):

- **"I saw pieces. I didn't know for certain."** (Honest yes.) Reeve nods once, slowly. **Reeve:** "Of course you did. That's what Liaisons are for." Not cruel. Stating a fact. She turns away. Door to trust: **open.**
- **"No, Dr. Callender. I didn't."** (Protective no.) She watches their face. She is not fooled. **Reeve:** "All right, Mr. Teague. Thank you for the escort." She turns away. Door: **closed.**
- **"I saw pieces. I didn't put them together. I'm sorry."** (Systemic truth.) She steps closer. **Reeve:** "You and I are the only people in this vault who see all four floors. We should have seen it. I didn't either." Door: **fully open.** This is the deepest entry into Reeve's trust available this week.
- **Silence / shrug / refusal to engage.** She waits, then nods. **Reeve:** "I understand. Thank you for the escort." Door: **closed, but quietly, without hostility.**

The protagonist returns to the Liaison corridor. Mae has dinner ready. No one asks what happened.

**18:00** — Dinner. Quiet vault.

**20:00** — Evening. If the protagonist visits Level 4, Nessa is cleaning the Galley. She saw them earlier. She has questions. **This is the first Nessa opening.**

**22:00** — Lights out. In 4b-11, Reeve does not sleep.
