## DAY 2 — TUESDAY — FIRST ANOMALY

The rumor begins. The discrepancy sharpens.

**06:30** — Wake.

**08:00** — Morning Address. Wren announces the petition will be read tomorrow. Still no name attached. Whispers move through the Hymn Hall afterward.

**08:30–11:30** — Morning runs. One takes the protagonist past Reeve's classroom. Reeve is teaching Reese Hoad at a desk; Linnet grades papers in the back. Reeve's chalkboard is now: *"Tuesday. If consent cannot be withdrawn, it is not consent. — Rousseau, paraphrased."*

**11:30** — Delivery to Dr. Oke in the Tier 1 Dining Hall. The protagonist overhears a fragment from the next table:

**Miriam:** "— sustained for over a year, documented, egregious —"

**Dr. Oke's nurse:** "Is that so?"

**Miriam:** "They were preparing Tier 3 candidates for Advancement. Without authorization. One expects better of an instructor of that standing."

*No name yet. The protagonist can infer.*

**12:00** — Lunch. Linnet sits near the protagonist. She talks about Reese Hoad — unaware of the morning's rumor.

**Linnet:** "He's so close, he'll test into the Advancement track next year, you should see how fast he's working."

Her face is bright.

**13:30** — **The Tribunal petition is posted** on the public board in the Level 1 Atrium:

> **PETITION FOR ADVANCEMENT REVIEW — DEMOTION PROCEEDING**
> Subject: Dr. Reeve Callender, Tier 1b, General Education
> Petitioner: The Senior Representative
> Charge: Unauthorized expansion of instructional scope with prejudicial effect on tier integrity.
> Hearing: Tomorrow, 14:00. Tribunal Chamber.

By 14:00, every tier knows.

**14:30** — Reese Hoad is called out of class by his work supervisor. His mother is waiting. She tells him the sessions are over, effective immediately. Reese asks why. Mira does not answer. He asks if Reeve is in trouble.

**Mira:** "Come home after dinner, dear."

*This scene happens off-screen from the protagonist's perspective. They may hear about it at dinner if they are in the cafeteria.*

**17:00** — Reeve visits Camille Wren in the Archive. The conversation lasts seven minutes. Reeve asks what the Archive records about past demotions. Camille shows her. Reeve thanks her and leaves. Witnessed only if the protagonist is in the Archive at this hour.

**18:00** — Dinner. The cafeteria is quiet. Tier 3 whispers. Tier 1 eats in its own hall (no witnesses). Tier 4 on its own floor.

**20:00** — Evening. If the protagonist visits the Hymn Hall, Wren Gallagher and Camille Wren are rehearsing Sunday's formal address. Not load-bearing unless engaged. If the protagonist visits Reeve's suite (1b-09) on some pretext, she will open the door, look at them once, and say:

**Reeve:** "Thank you, Mr. Teague, but I have no need for a Liaison this evening."

Polite. Closed.

**22:00** — Lights out.

**Day 2 shape:** Demotion is now formally announced. Reeve knows. Reese partially knows. Protagonist has three data points (Monday's ledger, Miriam's words, the posted petition). Mira's face at the Monday note is now legible in retrospect.
