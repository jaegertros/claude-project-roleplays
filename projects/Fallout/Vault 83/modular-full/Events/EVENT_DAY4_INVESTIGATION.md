## DAY 4 — THURSDAY — INVESTIGATION

The protagonist has data and the option to use it. The day is structured around investigative and relational opportunities.

**06:30** — Wake.

**08:00** — Morning Address. Wren announces the sanitation apprenticeship schedule (Reeve's). Announces the weekend's Founding Day ceremonies — Saturday dinner (18:00), Sunday formal address (11:00). Attendance required.

**08:30 — The first investigative window.** Morning assignment includes returning to Harrin Keep's office to retrieve the Monday ledger from Wren Gallagher's desk. Harrin is not in — his assistant Lee (Tier 2c) hands the ledger over. The protagonist can check the page they saw Monday. **Cal Hoad's entry has been corrected** — *"NOT AVAILABLE"* has been replaced with *"AVAILABLE — REVIEW NEXT CYCLE"* in Harrin's own hand. The discrepancy has been hidden in the up-going copy. The duplicate in Tier 3's files (still downstairs) has not been touched. **The protagonist can go verify this.** If they do, they observe: the pre-cover-up evidence remains in the Tier 3 copy. They have now confirmed a small cover-up related to the week's events.

This is **DEPTH 1 → 2.** Physical investigation. Cross-referencing records. Confirmation that the system was pre-arranged.

**10:00 — The Verity delivery.** The protagonist is assigned to deliver a handwritten sympathy card from Miriam Callender to Verity Callender-Voss (1c-18). The card references "the arrangements" and "this difficult week."

Verity opens the door personally instead of sending the Tier 1c aide. She reads the card in the doorway, face still, and looks up:

**Verity:** "Do you know what this is about, Mr. Teague?"

- **Yes** (direct): She holds the protagonist's gaze for a moment. Steps back. **Verity:** "Come in. Please." Tea is offered. **Verity route opens.**
- **"I'm not permitted to know, ma'am"**: She thanks them, closes the door politely. **Verity route narrows significantly.**
- **"It's about your birthday"** (literal, deflecting): **Verity:** "Yes. Of course." Closes the door. **Route narrows.**
- **"It's about the housing"**: She goes still. **Verity:** "You know." Pause. **Verity:** "Come in, Mr. Teague. I'd like to hear what else you know." **Route opens — differently than 'Yes' opens it. She is now engaged on the uncomfortable fact, not on sympathy.**

**12:00** — Lunch. Cafeteria or Tier 1 Dining Hall.

**13:00 — The Reeve visit (conditional).** The protagonist can descend to Level 4. Reeve is in 4b-11 — the sanitation apprenticeship does not start until Day 4 afternoon training. She is sitting on the bed. Has not left the room since yesterday. Has not eaten. If the protagonist brings her food (from the cafeteria or Galley), she will accept. If they bring nothing but presence, she accepts that too. **Reeve route deepens.**

**14:00 — Nessa's Galley (conditional).** If the protagonist is on Level 4 at lunch or afterward, Nessa is in the Galley prepping dinner. She will feed them without being asked. She sits across the table and asks:

**Nessa:** "So who sent you, love? Nobody sends Liaisons here."

Response opens the Nessa route:
- If the protagonist says **"Reeve"** — Nessa tells them what Level 4 has been saying about the new arrival (cautious, not unkind, watchful).
- If **"Nobody"** — she tells them to eat and talk to her anyway.
- If **"I wanted to see Level 4"** — she laughs, genuine and warm. **Nessa:** "Well, love, welcome. Sit."

**15:30 — Linnet's classroom (conditional).** Linnet is in the reading classroom grading papers alone. Face red around the eyes. Composing a letter to Reeve she cannot deliver. If the protagonist offers to deliver it, she accepts. She cries briefly. **Linnet route opens.**

**17:00 — Camille Wren (conditional).** If the protagonist visits the Archive, Camille has been waiting. She will ask, offhandedly, whether the protagonist has been thinking about the vault's founding. **This is the first DEPTH 2 → 3 opportunity.** If they press, she says:

**Camille:** "I have wondered for thirty years about one specific thing in the founding records. I have never asked anyone. I don't suppose you'd want to tell me what you've been thinking about."

What the protagonist says here determines whether Camille opens further on Day 5.

**18:00** — Dinner. Reeve eats her first meal in the Level 4 Galley. Nessa sets a bowl in front of her.

**Nessa:** "Eat, love."

She walks away. Reeve eats.

**20:00** — Evening. The protagonist chooses who to prioritize: Reeve, Verity, Linnet, Samia, Beatrix, Camille, Irena. Multi-NPC cultivation is feasible at this stage but will narrow as the week closes.

**22:00** — Lights out.

**Day 4 shape:** Physical investigation is open. Five romance paths have entry points. Camille is the most explicit DEPTH route. Ledger access is not yet reachable — location and password both unknown to the protagonist.
