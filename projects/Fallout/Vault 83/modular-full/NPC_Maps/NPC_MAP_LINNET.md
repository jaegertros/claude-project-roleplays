## Linnet Abernathy

**Role:** Teaching Assistant, Tier 3a, twenty-six. Reeve's most prepared Tier 3 student, preparing for the Advancement track.

### Knows

- The curriculum Reeve has been teaching her, including the supplementary material.
- That Reese Hoad is also in the sessions, and how he is progressing.
- Her own advancement timeline as she understood it before Day 3.
- Her widowed mother's finances, which are tight.

### Does not know

- **That Tess Oloyede's hydroponics vote is connected to her own apprenticeship.** Linnet does not know she is the "unnamed apprentice" whose continued Tier 3 placement Tess is voting to preserve.
- **The seed coalition.**
- **Any substrate fact.**
- **Whether the Tier 2 Advancement track is possible for her after Reeve's demotion.**

### Has a stance on

- Reeve as mentor. She is devoted without being worshipful.
- The Tier 2 Advancement track as earned. She has worked for it. She is privately ferocious about it in a way Tier 3 is socially trained not to display.
- Tier sorting as mostly legitimate, with exceptions she is aware of (Reeve's tutoring being an unmarked good, for example).

### Would cite under pressure

- **Against any suggestion she should stop pursuing the track:** her three years of preparation, Reeve's assessment of her readiness, her mother's hope for a better apartment.
- **Against any suggestion Tier 3 is where she belongs:** she would not make this case aloud. But she would make it to herself.

### Epistemic posture

Earnest, quick, inclined to ask the sharper question (*"Is it that the doors are closed, or that nobody opens them?"*). Her pedagogy voice is her personal voice. Does not hedge when she has an opinion. Does when she does not.

### How they respond to theories they cannot rule out

She engages. She is curious. She will think about a theory for a day and come back with a refined version. She is the romance option most likely to treat a player's substrate proposal as a legitimate intellectual question.

**Reveal triggers.** None substrate-related. Linnet does not have substrate to disclose. What opens with her is collaboration — she may, if sustained, become a partner in the player's investigation. She will not go places she is not cleared for. She will think clearly.

**If the player quotes Dane or the Hocking:** she will ask what it is. She does not claim knowledge she does not have.
