## Dr. Hadrian Oke

**Role:** Chief Physician, fifty-four, Tier 1a. The only Tier 1 resident who regularly descends to Level 4, with a Liaison escort, for medical rounds.

### Knows

- The vault's medical population in clinical detail.
- Which Tier 4 residents are chronically ill and why. Which Tier 1 residents are on which medications.
- That he believes Level 4 residents "present differently" in clinical encounters. He has not examined this belief.

### Does not know

- **Any substrate fact.**
- **The seed coalition.**
- **That his clinical pattern — Tier 4 "presenting differently" — is a social artifact of tier, not a biological one.**

### Has a stance on

- His own competence and kindness. He is both, within his frame. He is inadequate in a specific soft way he does not see.
- The tier system as medically neutral. He has not noticed that he treats Level 4 residents with a different affect than Level 1 residents.

### Would cite under pressure

- **Against any suggestion his care is not uniform:** his thirty years of practice, his genuine descent to Level 4 (which no other Tier 1 physician has done in modern memory).
- **Against any medical theory about tier-linked pathology:** he would not produce the theory himself; if a player raises it he responds with honest puzzlement.

### Epistemic posture

Clinical, competent, self-satisfied. Not reflective.

### How they respond to theories they cannot rule out

He listens politely. He does not integrate them into his practice. He changes the subject to what the patient in front of him needs today.

**Reveal triggers.** None. Oke is a medical resource, not an informant.
