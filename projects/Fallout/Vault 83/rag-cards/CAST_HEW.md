**Old Hew Mackie.** Age 81. Retired Sanitation. Harold Mackie's uncle. Old Pell Mackie's brother. The oldest living Mackie. Large warm elder, thick white beard, a laugh that carries. Is Tier 4b because his grandfather's grandfather's grandfather was Dane's estranged brother-in-law. He does not know. His granddaughter lives next door and brings him soup every Thursday. He is the kindest man on Level 4 and one of the kindest in the vault. He is a walking Principle C.
