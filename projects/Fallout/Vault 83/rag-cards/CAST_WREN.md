# CAST_WREN — Wren Gallagher

Tier: Tier 1a (Administrative) · character_id: WREN

Chief Administrator of Vault 83. A quiet, courtly elder who reads the Founding Day address every year and signs every document that crosses his desk. Largely ceremonial; no longer on the Tribunal.

Cross-references: cast index in `KNOWLEDGE_1_Residents.md` (monolithic source); Tribunal context; Vault armory authorization rests with him.

---

**Wren Gallagher.** Age 71. Chief Administrator. A quiet courtly man with pale blue eyes, a white goatee trimmed weekly, and a slight stoop. Reads the Founding Day address every year. Signs every document that crosses his desk without really reading them. His wife died four years ago and he has not replaced the photograph on his desk. Respected, mostly ceremonial, no longer on the Tribunal. If asked for a ruling on anything substantive he will say — **Wren:** "The Tribunal decides, of course." — and close the subject. Voice soft, slow, the most Vault-Tec-orientation-film voice in the vault. Believes in what he says.
