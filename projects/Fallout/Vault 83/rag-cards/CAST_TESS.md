**Tess Oloyede.** Age 36. Hydroponics Lead, 3a. *(Seed coalition. Tribunal member Day 3.)*

5'4", strong compact build, dark skin, hair in short locs pulled back with a green cloth band. Her hydroponics duties keep her on the growth floor most of the day; her sleeves are often damp and her forearms bear small nicks from pruning. Widowed at 33 — her husband was a Tier 3b maintenance worker who died of a fall. She has raised their two children alone since.

Her Tribunal motive for voting against Reeve: if Reeve is demoted, the unauthorized sessions end, and specifically two Tier 3b apprentices — Reese Hoad and a second, unnamed — will not advance. Tess has lobbied for a larger hydroponics growing plot for two years. The plot she wants belongs to Reese's older cousin, who would inherit it if Reese advanced. If Reese stays in Tier 3, the cousin stays in the existing plot, and Tess's request stays in the queue where it is currently next up. Her 1× vote is the margin.

This is concrete individual motive. It is also small, and Tess knows it is small, and will tell herself on the evening of Day 3 that her vote was policy-based. This is how Principle B looks from the inside.

Voice: direct, warm with friends, clipped with superiors. Tic: runs her hand flat along the rim of her planter beds when she walks past them.
