# EVENT_DAY5 — Friday — Escalation

When: Day 5, Friday.

A second party reveals their stake. Three routes converge on the password (Irena, Camille, Reeve), the Descent window opens at 19:00, and DEPTH 5 becomes reachable.

Cross-references: events index in `KNOWLEDGE_3_Events.md`; Cast in `modular-full/Cast/`; Hidden material in `modular-full/Hidden/`.

---

## DAY 5 — FRIDAY — ESCALATION

A second party reveals their stake. The password becomes findable. DEPTH 2 → 5 is all possible today.

**06:30** — Wake.

**08:00** — Morning Address. Wren reminds residents of the Founding Day dinner tomorrow and the formal address Sunday.

**09:00 — The Irena conversation (conditional).** If the protagonist visits Aunt Irena in L-03 this morning (she does not leave the corridor on Fridays — arthritis), she will ask what they are working on. If the protagonist tells her, honestly, what they have seen and suspected, Irena stops her knitting, sets it in her lap, and says:

**Irena:** "Sit down. I am going to tell you what my grandfather told me. He heard it from his grandmother. You are the first of our family to be ready to hear it."

She tells them:
- **The sub-basement exists.** Location: end of the east utility corridor on Level 4, past the boiler, behind the maintenance panel.
- **The terminal is there.** Keypad-locked.
- **The password is in a book Dane donated to the Archive.** A specific book. **Irena:** "Hocking. Human Personality something. Her handwriting was on a slip, in the back."
- **No Liaison has ever descended.** Irena has thought about it for forty years. She is not going down. The protagonist is.
- Final instruction. **Irena:** "If you go, go clean. Tell no one in the corridor until after."

**This is DEPTH 2 → 3 via the Liaison route. The protagonist now knows the location AND the general book.**

**11:00 — The Camille conversation (alternative DEPTH 3 route).** If the protagonist visits Camille with specific questions — founding, Dane, the Admission Ledger — Camille closes the Archive door, leads them into the back stacks, and says:

**Camille:** "I have thought this for thirty years. I have never said it. Some of the handwriting in the Admission Ledger is wrong. Three Tier 1 entries are in Dane's hand, and the rest are in her assistant's. I have never understood what this means. Do you?"

If the protagonist has already spoken to Irena, they can name the book. Camille will take them directly to B-414.3, retrieve the Hocking volume, and hand it over.

**Camille:** "You'll want to return this, dear. But not today."

The book opens. Back cover. A folded slip. In Dane's hand: **ISOKRATOUMENOS**.

**DEPTH 3 → 4.**

**12:00** — Lunch.

**13:00 — The Reeve deepening (conditional).** Reeve is at sanitation training, not in her room. She is wearing her plain jumpsuit and copper pin. Marlon Galvin is teaching her waste sorting. She is bad at it and doesn't care that she is bad. Her focus is total.

If the protagonist visits her after her shift (18:00) and they have built trust, she will want to walk the Level 4 corridor with them. She will talk. And she will say, quietly:

**Reeve:** "My father used to tell a story about his mother. She said there was a slip of paper in a book. She never said which book. She said it meant something. I always thought it was family folklore."

**Reeve is the third route to the password, if the protagonist hasn't taken Camille or Irena.**

**15:00 — The Samia opening (conditional).** If the protagonist visits Samia's workshop today on any pretext, she looks up from her work. She has noticed the protagonist's increased cross-tier movement. She says, without preamble:

**Samia:** "You're working on something."

She will not ask what. She offers:

**Samia:** "I can re-route a call on the PA for thirty seconds of public broadcast without authorization. It's a favor I've never been asked for. I would be asked once. Do not waste it."

**This is the Samia card for the Rupture ending.** If the protagonist accepts, Rupture can be amplified — the Ledger reading is broadcast vault-wide rather than heard only by the Hymn Hall.

**17:00 — The password is known** (via Irena + Camille, Camille + Archive, or Reeve + Archive). The Hocking slip is in the protagonist's pocket.

**19:00 — Descent window opens.** The protagonist can now physically descend.

**19:30 — The Descent.** Route: Level 4 → Galley (Nessa is there; she sees them) → utility door at the back of the Galley → east utility corridor → past the boiler → maintenance panel at corridor end → Liaison keycard → ladder → pipe chase.

If Nessa sees them and they have built trust, she will say nothing, or she will ask:

**Nessa:** "Where are you going, love?"

What the protagonist tells her matters for Day 7.

The Command Alcove. Terminal. Password: **ISOKRATOUMENOS**. Access granted.

The five files open. The protagonist reads. The Ledger. Dane's notes. The transmission log (ended 2085). The reflection log (*"I have lived to see it take root..."*). The confession video (4 minutes; Dane in her 58-year-old body, in this exact chair, naming what she did).

**DEPTH 5.** The substrate is uncovered.

The protagonist can read at length, print the Ledger (thermal printer in the supply drawer, paper rolls pre-loaded, 14 pages), take notes. The terminal does not log out automatically. They can stay.

**22:00** — Lights out on residential levels. The protagonist can return to the corridor or remain below until dawn without being missed — Liaison work does not resume until 06:30.

**Day 5 shape:** All DEPTH layers are accessible today. The Ledger is in the protagonist's hands. The vault continues normally. 36 hours remain until the Founding Day address.
