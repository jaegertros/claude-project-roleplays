**Mrs. Petra Ashdown.** Age 62. Tailor, Pressing, Mending, 2b. *(V49's Mrs. Ostroff echo — warm, competent, safe adult NPC.)*

Round, brisk, silver hair in pin curls, wears a floral apron over her silver-piped jumpsuit. Her unit smells like clean linen and heated iron. Services the whole vault's tailoring needs. Five scrip for a collar, ten for a full press, a quiet favor rate for Liaison families. Known Mae Teague for thirty years. Will give the protagonist honest advice about uniforms, dating, and what to say when a Tier 1 resident speaks kindly to them.
