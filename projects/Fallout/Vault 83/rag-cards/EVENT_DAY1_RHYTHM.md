# EVENT_DAY1 — Monday — Rhythm

## DAY 1 — MONDAY — RHYTHM

Normal Liaison day. Three plants. No action required.

**06:30** — Wake in the Teague apartment. Pip-Boy on the bedside table, gray jumpsuit on the hook.

**06:55** — Breakfast in the corridor kitchen. Mae Teague has made porridge. Ezra reviews the day's run list. Beatrix drifts in from L-04 with her hair half-up.

**Beatrix:** "Teag — actually, did you eat? There's porridge. Sorry, I mean, there's porridge if you want it."

**07:30** — Report to Ezra for assignments. Three runs today. List handed over on folded paper.

**07:45** — Morning Hymn. Standard attendance.

**08:00** — Morning Address. Wren Gallagher announces Dr. Oke's fall medical rounds on Tier 4. He also announces, without emphasis, that the Tribunal will convene Wednesday for a petition review. No name attached. The residents who have been following rumors already know. The residents who have not do not notice.

**08:30 — Run 1.** Sealed medical requisition from Dr. Oke (Level 1) to pharmacy (Level 3). Passes Camille Wren in the Archive corridor.

**Camille:** "Good morning, dear."

**10:00 — Run 2 (THE PLANT).** Carry a requisition ledger from Harrin Keep's clerk's office (Tier 2b) to Wren Gallagher's office (Tier 1a). Harrin is mid-review; the ledger is open to a working page. The protagonist walks it up two levels. In the east corridor or elevator, they glance at the page:

- **Cal Hoad's** Tier 3b apprenticeship application: marked *"NOT AVAILABLE THIS CYCLE"* in blue pen.
- Two pages earlier, the duplicate entry (intended for Tier 3 records): marked *"AVAILABLE — REVIEW NEXT CYCLE"* in a different hand.

Subtle. Explicable as clerical error if the protagonist doesn't dwell. But the hands are different, the dates identical, and one version is going up while the other stays in Tier 3's files. The protagonist closes the ledger before entering Wren's office. Wren signs the transmittal slip without opening the ledger. It sits on his desk until Harrin collects it.

*The protagonist has perceived something. No action required.*

**11:30 — Run 3.** A folded note from Eldon Callender-Voss's office to Mira Hoad's dental suite. Eldon writes it in his own hand (unusual — he normally dictates). The envelope is sealed. Mira opens it in front of the protagonist, reads it, folds it.

**Mira:** "Thank you, dear. Tell his office it's fine."

Her face is careful.

**12:00** — Lunch at the Tier 2/3 cafeteria. Linnet Abernathy greets the protagonist, makes a small joke.

**Linnet:** "Sunny again."

(Vault weather is constant; the joke is a Tier 3 verbal tradition.)

**13:30 — Run 4.** Small delivery to Camille Wren in the Archive. One of Mrs. Ashdown's old tailoring manuals. Camille thanks them, asks after Ezra, offers tea. If accepted, she speaks with the protagonist for ten minutes about nothing in particular. Her attention is high. She is testing the water.

**15:00** — Free time. Workshop Commons, corridor, or a passage to Level 4 if the protagonist invents a reason. Reeve Callender's classroom is passable on the way — her chalkboard reads *"A social contract requires consent. Define 'consent' as the contract assumes it."*

**18:00** — Dinner in the corridor or cafeteria.

**20:00** — Evening. Ezra plays cribbage with Gideon Wright in the common area. Beatrix reads on the kitchen counter, swinging one foot. If the protagonist sits with her she will talk — about nothing, about everything. If they go to Irena's apartment, Irena tells a minor family story (the big ones are reserved for later days).

**22:00** — Lights out.

**Day 1 plants deposited:**
- The ledger discrepancy (Cal Hoad's application, two versions)
- The sealed note from Eldon to Mira
- Reeve's chalkboard sentence
- Camille's deliberate attention

No action required of the protagonist. All plants will germinate later.
