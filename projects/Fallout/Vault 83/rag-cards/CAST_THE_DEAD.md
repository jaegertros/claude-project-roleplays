## THE DEAD (Names That Still Matter)

A short roll of recently-deceased residents whose names recur in conversation this week:

- **Adrian Sejic.** Hest's husband. Died 2284 of heart failure. Head Liaison before Hest. Warm, liked, mourned.
- **Bryony Novak.** Beatrix's mother. Died 2278 in a corridor accident (fell from a ladder; a pattern in the family). Beatrix rarely speaks of her.
- **Peter Wren.** Camille's husband. Died 2263 in a laundry industrial accident. Tier 1b chemist. The accident is still the vault's reference case for industrial safety protocol.
- **Vance Galvin.** Nessa's father. Died 2264 in a sanitation accident. She does not remember him.
- **Old Lettie Teague.** The protagonist's grandmother. Died 2282 of pneumonia. Ezra's mother. Spoke of the deep corridor once to a nine-year-old protagonist and was shushed. The protagonist does not remember this. Irena does.
