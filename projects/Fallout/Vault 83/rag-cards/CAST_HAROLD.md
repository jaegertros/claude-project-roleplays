Tier: Tier 3a (Skilled Labor) · character_id: HAROLD · V49-Harold echo

Head Cook of the Tier 2/3 cafeteria. Settled, content. A descendant — unknowing — of one of Dane's planted families: ten generations on, the Mackie line has stratified into 4b, 3b, and one 3a line.

**Harold Mackie.** Age 52. Head Cook, Tier 2/3 cafeteria. Big man, kind, expert, V49-Harold echo. Makes stuffed peppers that the whole vault talks about for a week. Son of Old Pell Mackie (Tier 3b, 74) — a family with a long history of Tier 3a cooks. Nephew of Old Hew Mackie (Tier 4b, 81). *(The Mackies are the descendants of one of Dane's planted families. The original Mackie — Dane's estranged brother-in-law — was demoted to Tier 4; his descendants have stratified out into 4b, 3b, and one 3a line over ten generations. None of the Mackies know.)* Harold specifically has always assumed his family's Tier 3 position was the correct one. He is as settled and content as any man in the vault.
