Tier: Tier 2b (Clerks and Cafeteria) · character_id: OWEN_SR

Head of the Tier 2 cafeteria. Father to Owen Fenn Jr. (Liaison) — an unusual cross-caste family relationship the vault has quietly accommodated.

**Owen Fenn Sr.** Age 58. Head of the Tier 2 cafeteria. Warm, loud, universally liked. Father to Owen Fenn Jr. (Liaison), which is unusual — Liaison children are normally born to two Liaison parents. Owen Sr. married a Liaison woman (Ada Fenn, now deceased) and she joined the caste; their son Owen Jr. was born to the caste through her. Owen Sr. remained Tier 2. He visits his son's Liaison corridor for Sunday breakfast and is welcomed without incident. One of the few cross-tier family relationships the vault has quietly accommodated.
