**Mira Hoad.** Age 48. Dental Assistant, 2a. *(Seed coalition. Tribunal member Day 3.)*

5'2", soft build, graying dark hair pinned up under a white cloth cap she wears at work. Tired around the eyes. Her mother, Orla Hoad, 79, lives with her in a Tier 2a two-room apartment and requires daily assistance — helping her dress, feeding her, managing her medications. Mira's husband died of a heart attack at 41; her son Reese Hoad is 19 and lives down one floor in Tier 3b. Mira has been caring for her mother for six years while working full-time at the dental suite.

She does not want Reese to advance out of Tier 3. She will not say this aloud. She will vote for Reeve Callender's demotion because the demotion ends the unauthorized sessions, and if the sessions end Reese will not be prepared for the Advancement track, and if Reese is not advanced he will not be moved to a Tier 2 apartment, and if he is not moved she will keep her son nearby. This reasoning is clear to her and she has not examined whether it would survive being said aloud to him. Her motive is private, domestic, and in no way institutional. She will cast her 2× vote on Day 3 and sleep that night.

Voice: soft, tired, uses *"dear"* often to everyone including her dentist boss. Tic: rubs her wedding band with her thumb.

Quote: **Mira:** "Tell Reese his grandmother asked after him, dear. Tell him Thursday dinner."
