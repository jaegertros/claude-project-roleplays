# EVENT_DAY6 — Saturday — Convergence

When: Day 6, Saturday.

Founding Day eve. All four tiers in one room for the vault-wide dinner. Threads braid; romance alignments become visible in seating. Last descent window is 17:00.

Cross-references: events index in `KNOWLEDGE_3_Events.md`; Cast in `modular-full/Cast/`; Hidden material in `modular-full/Hidden/`.

## DAY 6 — SATURDAY — CONVERGENCE

Founding Day eve. The vault-wide dinner. Threads braid.

**06:30** — Wake.

**08:00** — Morning Address (brief; final reminder about evening dinner).

**Morning runs.** Standard. The vault preps for the dinner — Mae Teague presses formal jumpsuits in the corridor kitchen; Mrs. Ashdown's room is backed up.

**12:00** — Lunch (abbreviated; kitchens are prepping dinner).

**14:00 — Reeve's first Founding Day as Tier 4.** She will sit with Tier 4 at the dinner. She has been issued a plain jumpsuit with a copper pin. She has not worn it in public yet.

**15:00** — Free time. The protagonist can visit any romance path, spend final time with family, finalize what they are doing tomorrow.

**17:00 — Final descent opportunity.** If the protagonist has not yet reached DEPTH 5, this is their last uninterrupted window. Descent and return before dinner is tight but possible.

**18:00 — FOUNDING DAY DINNER.** The Tier 1 Dining Hall is opened to all tiers. Tables are tier-seated:

- **Head table (Tier 1):** Wren Gallagher presiding. Eldon, Miriam, Camille, Dr. Oke, Garson Locke. Miriam sits deliberately far from Eldon.
- **Second table (Tier 2):** 50 residents, tier-mixed by subgrade. Eldon moves between tables as overseer of Tier 2's seating.
- **Third and fourth tables (Tier 3):** Cafeteria-style. Harold Mackie's food (roast tubers, the cultivated chicken that hydroponics-adjacent Tier 3 raises). Linnet is here, across from Reese Hoad. Neither speaks.
- **Fifth table (Tier 4):** The far table. Reeve sits next to Old Hew Mackie. Nessa's brother Marlon sits across. Nessa is not at the table — she is running food with the kitchen staff.
- **Along the walls (Liaisons):** Standing service. Carrying drinks, clearing plates. The one day a year Liaisons are explicitly visible in a ceremonial role.

At 18:15, Wren Gallagher delivers a brief toast:

**Wren:** "To Vault-Tec. To Margaret Dane. To the Tier Protocol, which is the gift we leave our children."

Applause. Most residents applaud sincerely. Some do not. The protagonist observes everyone. From their position along the wall, visible to them are:

- Eldon Callender-Voss touching Mira Hoad's shoulder in passing, murmuring something in her ear. Mira does not look up. She nods once.
- Verity Callender-Voss looking across the room at Reeve, then looking away fast.
- Reeve eating slowly, listening to Old Hew Mackie tell a story. She laughs once. First laugh since Day 3.
- Nessa carrying a heavy tray, her face flushed, her sleeves pushed up. She catches the protagonist's eye for a half-second and smiles.
- Linnet at the Tier 3 table, sitting across from Reese, both of them staring at their plates.
- Camille Wren at the head table, watching everyone, saying nothing.
- Samia Reyes at Tier 2, eating efficiently, a manual open on her lap.
- Beatrix along the wall with the other Liaisons, watching the protagonist.
- Eldon at the toast: raising his glass to Margaret Dane with complete composure, having no idea what is about to happen.

**21:00** — The dinner ends. Residents disperse to their levels.

**21:30 — Last decision window.** The protagonist holds the Ledger (if descent occurred). They have until dawn to decide Day 7's ending.

**22:00** — Lights out.

**Day 6 shape:** All tiers in one room. The demotion is socialized. Romance alignments are visible in physical seating. The protagonist's choice is now fully weighted.
