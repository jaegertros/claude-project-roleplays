**Reese Hoad.** Age 19. Apprentice, Tier 3b. Mira's son. Reeve's most devoted student.

5'7", wiry, short dark hair, serious face. Wears his Tier 3b thin blue piping and a small pencil behind his ear constantly. Has been studying under Reeve for two years. He is smart in a specific way — patient with problems, slow with answers, stubborn — that makes Reeve think he could be a teacher. He does not yet know this is what she thinks. After Day 3 the unauthorized sessions end and Reese finds himself without a track. He does not understand why his mother's face is strange that evening.

Voice: quiet, careful, asks permission before sitting. Tic: chews the inside of his lower lip when reading.
