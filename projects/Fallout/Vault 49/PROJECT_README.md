## Setup (two minutes)

1. **Create a new Claude Project.** Call it whatever you like.
2. **Copy `PROJECT_INSTRUCTIONS.md` into the Project Instructions field.** Paste blind.
3. **Upload files 3 through 8** (the KNOWLEDGE files) as Project Knowledge. Upload blind.
4. **Open a new conversation in the project.** In a second tab or panel, open the tracker: paste `vault49-tracker.jsx` into an artifact and run it. It persists state between sessions.
5. **Say anything to begin.** The narrator will open cold.

## What's different this time

- **Fallout voice.** Dry humor, Vault-Tec cheerfulness laid over darker material, period Americana. Less Mann, more New Vegas.
- **Your character is vault-born.** Tenth generation. You have never seen the sky. You will not leave the vault in this story — this is not that kind of Fallout. It's claustrophobic on purpose.
- **Pip-Boy tracker.** The tracker is styled as a Pip-Boy and handles HP, radiation, scrip, inventory, and quest notes. Green CRT, all the trimmings.
- **Disciplined loot.** Items only go on the Pip-Boy if they matter narratively. No 47-bottle-caps-and-a-roll-of-tape clutter. A specific pistol in a specific drawer matters. A handful of found junk does not.
- **Four alternate openings.** Chosen randomly by the narrator each fresh conversation. Same vault, same cast, different dramatic entry points. Good for replay. If you want to force a specific one, say so in your first message ("give me opening B" or "the maintenance one").
- **The week has the same rhythm you liked** — seven days, scripted beats, a final day where everything converges, the same "four endings, no editorial verdict" structure. The mystery is discovered, not served.

---

## How to play

- Write in first or third person — the narrator will match you.
- Responses stay tight: 80–150 words. You drive the scene.
- The Pip-Boy tracker at the end of every response tells you what changed. Mirror it into the artifact as you go.
- Nothing comes easily. The narrator will never volunteer the strange things. If you want to find something, look. If you want to know something, ask.
- Romance is available with multiple characters. Same literary handling as the other projects.
- The vote happens whether you engage with it or not. Your influence is in what you do between 08:01 and 17:29.
