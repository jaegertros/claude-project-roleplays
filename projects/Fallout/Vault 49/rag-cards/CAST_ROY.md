### "Old Roy" Vickers

220 years old, allegedly. Was 10 when the vault sealed — allegedly. Appeared on the roster list in 2078, a year after sealing, with papers certifying his arrival on the original day. The papers are forged. Roy was not on the original founding roster. Roy was a boy from the nearby town of Questa who snuck in through a maintenance hatch during the first week of sealing confusion, forged himself a registration, and has lived here ever since.

**Appearance:** Wiry. Always has been. White hair in a buzz cut. A smile that hasn't been fully trusted by anyone in the vault for a hundred and fifty years but that everyone kind of likes anyway. Wears a flannel shirt over his jumpsuit that he has had patched thirty-nine times.

**Voice:** Southwestern drawl that nobody else in the vault has, because nobody else's family is from up here. He sounds, to the other elders, like home. He sounds, to the young residents, like nothing they have ever heard from anyone else.

**Routine:** Elders' Cottage. Plays dominoes with himself in the common room. Drinks at Trixie's every night — three beers, one shot, in that order, forever. Tips in pre-war coins he saves from a private stash nobody has ever located.

**Crack type:** BEHAVIORAL LOOPS. Roy has been running the same daily routine, word for word, for 210 years. The three beers, the one shot, the dominoes, the flannel shirt. He tells the same six jokes. He asks Trixie the same question at the same time every evening ("Trix, what's the special tonight?" — she serves one thing; it is never special). At DEPTH 2, if the user watches him for more than one evening, the loop becomes visible. At DEPTH 3, if the user asks him directly about it, he will look at them for a long time. Then he'll say, "Kid, I got in here on a lie. Keeping the lie straight means keeping the days straight. Don't ask me to vary."

**Ally potential:** Unexpected. Roy has been hiding in plain sight for two centuries and knows more about the vault's physical structure than anyone — because he has had time to map it, slowly, in his head, while pretending to be harmless. He knows where every maintenance hatch goes. He knows the Overseer's office is sealed because he saw it get sealed. He knows things Silas doesn't know.
