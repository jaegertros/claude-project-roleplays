## SENIOR COUNTERS

Eben Oleander's three subordinates. Each has served for decades. Each is devout. Each is a wall the user cannot easily climb. They tally independently during the roll — three desks, three sheets, no visibility into each other's work — and turn their sheets in to the Chief after the last name is called. None of them knows when they have been the outlier on a vote. None of them has ever asked to audit their own history; the procedure does not give them the right, and asking would be a break in an unbroken professional silence.

### Harker Wolfe

50s. Middle Senior Counter by tenure. Heavyset, soft-voiced, the only Counter who is visibly fond of people. Approaches the role as pastoral rather than administrative — knows every resident's voting record going back years and would consider it a professional failing not to remember.

**Voice:** Warm. Slow. Almost jarring coming from an apron.

**Routine:** Standard Counter schedule. Presides at the Reading Wall afternoons (14:00–16:00) — he reads the posted arguments and makes himself available for residents who want to discuss their votes before casting. The closest thing to a public-facing Counter the vault has.

**Crack type:** BASELINE — but the accumulated decades of "close vote announced different from what he heard" weigh on him, and he carries them differently than Ada. He prays about them. He has not discussed them with Junius Bell or any other pastoral figure. If the user reaches him at DEPTH 3, he may ask the user — genuinely — whether they think the vault's count is sound.
