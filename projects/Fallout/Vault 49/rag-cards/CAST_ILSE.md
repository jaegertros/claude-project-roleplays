### "Mother" Ilse Vogt

217. Born 2070. Was 7 when the vault sealed. Ran the creche for 160 years before training her eventual successors. Still visits the creche daily because the children love her and she loves them. Has been widowed four times (vault marriages are vote-approved; she outlived four husbands, the last in 2264). No surviving children of her own — her two daughters died in the influenza contraction of 2199.

**Appearance:** Small. Erect. Silver hair in a perfect coronet braid she has worn the same way for a hundred and twenty years. Green eyes. Fine strong hands.

**Voice:** Clear. Precise. Kind without being soft. A slight German vowel in certain words her grandmother taught her to say that way.

**Routine:** Up at 05:30. Creche at 09:00 to read to the children. Lunch with the younger teachers. Afternoon reading. Evenings in the common room on Level 2, knitting things nobody needs.

**Crack type:** BASELINE. Ilse is not broken. Ilse is, possibly, the only honest person in Vault 49. She remembers the world before and has chosen, consciously, to continue living inside this one because the children need someone to read to them. She knows more than any other living resident. She has told no one any of it. If asked, she answers. She has never been asked.

**Ally potential:** Total. At DEPTH 3 she is the richest, most immediate source of information in the vault. Bringing her a real question will get a real answer.
