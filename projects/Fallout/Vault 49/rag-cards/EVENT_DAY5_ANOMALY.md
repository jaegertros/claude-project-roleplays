## DAY 5 (FRIDAY) — THE ANOMALY

**The Morning Question:** *"Shall the Overseer's office be opened?"*

The ballot hall will go silent for a different reason than Tuesday. Speaker Dailey reads the question, stops, reads it again, and looks up at Chief Counter Oleander.

Eben Oleander rises.

"Speaker. This question is not in the Register. This question is not from the Query Box. This question is not a Necessity. This question has not been vetted. It will not be put to the vote."

The Speaker hesitates. Looks at the paper. Looks at Eben. Looks at the room.

She says, "The Chief Counter has ruled. The question is withdrawn."

She moves to the next agenda item — a routine Query Box question about whether the library should acquire a new shelf. The vote proceeds normally.

**But the first question was posed.** It was read aloud in the ballot hall. It is in the PA recording for the day. It happened.

**The day:** An undercurrent. The vault is talking about it. Nobody knows who submitted it. Nobody admits to having submitted it. The Counters are in their chamber all day with the door closed. Sergeant Bronski stations one of his officers in the corridor outside, unobtrusively.

- If the user asks Eben directly: "An error. Errors happen. They are corrected." He will not elaborate.
- If the user asks Speaker Dailey: She is shaken. She will meet with the user privately if they request it. She will say, "I've been Speaker for eight months. I've never seen a question arrive with no source. It came in through the morning queue like any other. It didn't have a submission tag." She will ask the user not to spread this. She is frightened. She is also curious.
- If the user asks Peg Unsworth: "Well, dear, someone's fed up. About time, too." She will wink.
- If the user asks Perrin: Perrin is not in the chamber today. She has been reassigned to the archive by Eben for "inventory duties." The user can find her there. Perrin will say, very quietly: "It was me."

(This is true, at DEPTH 3. Perrin submitted the question through an administrative back-channel she discovered in her training. She is, at this point in the week, committed to something she does not yet have the words for.)
