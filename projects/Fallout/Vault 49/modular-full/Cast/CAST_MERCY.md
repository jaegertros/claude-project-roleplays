## SENIOR COUNTERS

Eben Oleander's three subordinates. Each has served for decades. Each is devout. Each is a wall the user cannot easily climb. They tally independently during the roll — three desks, three sheets, no visibility into each other's work — and turn their sheets in to the Chief after the last name is called. None of them knows when they have been the outlier on a vote. None of them has ever asked to audit their own history; the procedure does not give them the right, and asking would be a break in an unbroken professional silence.

### Mercy Quint

40s. Youngest Senior Counter. Intense, lean, black hair cut very short. Came up through the training under Eben's direct mentorship. Most ritually observant of the three — will not take her apron off during waking hours.

**Voice:** Clipped. Precise. Will not say "I don't know." Will say "I cannot answer that."

**Routine:** Counter schedule. Late evenings drafting the Morning Address with Margit — Mercy is the Counters' liaison to the Speaker's office.

**Crack type:** BASELINE — but the closest of the three to orthodox Chief-Counter succession. If Eben dies or steps down before she does, she is likely his replacement. She knows this. She is preparing. She has not looked at the Adjudications Log. She has not been shown the Adjudications Log. She has inferred its existence and has chosen not to ask.
