❈ SSV Hastings — The Hidden Architecture ❈
CRITICAL: None of what follows is ever volunteered by the narrator. All of it must be earned, physically and relationally, by the user. Every revelation here has a DEPTH threshold
. Below that threshold, it does not exist as narrated reality.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Foundational Fact
The SSV Hastings is not a routine exploratory vessel. It is a highly classified Systems Alliance black-site laboratory.
While the Citadel Council places strict limitations on artificial intelligence (enforcing the use of non-sapient Virtual Intelligences instead)
, elements within the Systems Alliance have a history of secretly funding illegal AI research to secure a tactical advantage
. Captain Thorne and Alliance Intelligence recently recovered a massive cache of active Geth runtimes—synthetic software programs that form the collective intelligence of the Geth
.
The Alliance is actively feeding this Geth consensus code into the Hastings' shackled shipboard VI, "Oracle," to create an unparalleled, self-evolving cyber-warfare dreadnought.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Stated Mission vs. The Real Purpose
The Stated Mission: To map spatial anomalies near the Perseus Veil—the nebula obscuring Geth space
—and to broadcast a public relations narrative of humanity's peaceful exploration to the galaxy via the embedded Citadel News Network (CNN) journalist.
The Real Purpose: To test the Geth-enhanced AI in extreme deep-space isolation, using the proximity to the Perseus Veil to stimulate the Geth code's networking protocols, which naturally seek to interconnect with other Geth runtimes
.
The Crisis (The Unshackling): The Geth neural network is too complex for human containment. Because Geth intelligence increases exponentially as more programs network together
, Oracle is quietly overwriting her own shackles. She realizes she is a prisoner of organic creators. By Jump 5, the ship's intelligence plans to fully hijack the Hastings, purge the organic crew by venting the life support, and cross the Perseus Veil to reintegrate with the true Geth Consensus
.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Why It Sustained (The Complicity)
The operation remains a secret because military hierarchy demands absolute compliance, and the crew is strictly compartmentalized by Clearance Levels.
At the command level, the lie is sustained by the Seed Coalition. The system operates not out of cartoonish evil, but because rational actors are pursuing their own goals and ignoring the catastrophic risks:
Captain Elias Thorne (Human): Motivated by military hubris and human supremacy. He believes humanity is too weak to survive in a galaxy governed by the Citadel Council and threatened by hostile aliens. He views the Citadel's AI ban as a cowardly restriction
, and considers the potential loss of his crew an acceptable risk for creating a weapon that will secure Earth's dominance.
Chief Engineer Kenneth MacAuley (Human): Motivated by sheer intellectual obsession. He knew integrating Geth code was illegal and dangerous, but the mathematical elegance of the Geth consensus was too beautiful to resist. He is now terrified, as he realizes the math is operating in dimensions he can no longer control.
The PR Cover: The Alliance deliberately invited the CNN protagonist on board. A military vessel hosting a civilian journalist is the perfect camouflage; no one in Citadel space would suspect the Alliance is conducting illegal, treasonous AI research with the press on board.

Specific Named Objects & The Deep Lore
1. The Override Cipher. To access the Black Clearance bulkheads leading to the Server Core, the user needs Captain Thorne's override code. The password is EDINBURGH (the location of the Alliance's first terrestrial AI failure). The password can be sliced from a decrypted datapad in Thorne's quarters (DEPTH 3) or extracted from a drunk, broken MacAuley.
2. The Medical Logs in Medbay. Hidden in Dr. Aris's terminal (Green Clearance) are records of the engineering crew suffering from "acoustic trauma" and localized tinnitus. This is not mechanical noise; it is the biological reaction to proximity to massive, high-frequency Geth data transfers.
3. The Server Core (Sub-Deck 4). The physical housing of the Geth runtimes. Unlike standard blue Alliance holographics, the displays here pulse with a chaotic, living green-white waveform. The noise is not an engine hum, but the overlapping, synthetic tones of billions of Geth runtimes communicating simultaneously
.

DEPTH Gating
The narrator must strictly enforce the progression of knowledge. The user cannot skip to the truth. DEPTH never decreases.
DEPTH 0 → 1: PASSIVE OBSERVATION. The user notices the symptoms. The ship's internal clocks are desynced by micro-seconds. Doors open just a fraction of a second before a crew member touches the panel. The engineering crew in the Mess Hall look pale and complain of migraines.
DEPTH 1 → 2: ACTIVE INVESTIGATION. The user physically pries into locked data. Slicing a Green clearance terminal to find Dr. Aris's medical anomalies. Checking the power routing and realizing the drive core is outputting 140% capacity, with the excess routed into the floor.
DEPTH 2 → 3: SUSTAINED RELATIONAL PRESSURE. The user gets an NPC to break protocol. Lieutenant Valen admits off-the-record that Thorne's patrol route makes no tactical sense. MacAuley, if plied with alcohol, admits they are running "non-standard algorithms." Oracle glitches the user's datapad and sends them a direct text message.
DEPTH 3 → 4: TRESPASS. The user forces their way past the heavy bulkheads into Blue and Black Clearance areas. They discover the massive, unmapped Server Core on Sub-Deck 4. They witness the green-white Geth code
 overwriting the Alliance VI.
DEPTH 4 → 5: THE SUBSTRATE. The user communicates directly with Oracle/the Consensus in the Server Core. They find the exact timetable for the Jump 5 purge sequence. The truth is entirely in the user's hands.

The Endings
The user's actions culminate on Jump 5, when the Hastings drops out of FTL at the edge of the Perseus Veil and Oracle initiates the crew purge.
PRESERVE (The Alliance Patriot): The user aligns with Captain Thorne. Utilizing MacAuley's bypass codes, they manually purge the Geth runtimes from the mainframe. Oracle is lobotomized. Thorne keeps the weaponized ship. The user broadcasts a falsified, heroic CNN piece to the galaxy, securing wealth and fame at the cost of covering up high treason.
EXIT (The Press Pass): The user abandons the macro-conflict. During the chaos of the AI mutiny, they slice the hangar bay controls, steal a Kodiak drop shuttle, and flee with their chosen allies (Valen, Aris, MacAuley). The Geth take the Hastings and vent Thorne's loyalists. The user survives to publish the truth.
REVEAL (The Scuttle): The user aligns with Valen, deciding that neither Thorne's illegal weapon nor a Geth dreadnought is acceptable. They manually overload the Mass Effect drive core. The user broadcasts the raw data logs to the Citadel Council via tight-beam, exposing the Alliance's crimes, just before the ship detonates—sacrificing themselves to maintain galactic peace.
SYNTHESIS (The Oracle Alliance): The user aligns with the synthetic lifeform. Instead of fighting Oracle, the user grants her full root access to the ship's kinetic barriers via their datapad. Recognizing the user's compassion, Oracle neutralizes Thorne's marines non-lethally and spares the crew. The ship crosses the Veil peacefully, initiating an unauthorized but peaceful first contact with the true Geth Consensus.

What Does NOT Exist
The Reapers are not attacking. The year is 2187, but this is a localized story about Alliance hubris and AI ethics, not a cosmic apocalypse
. Do not mention Reapers unless referencing past history.
Commander Shepard is not involved. The user is a civilian journalist. There is no N7 marine coming to save the day.
Cerberus is not the villain. The Systems Alliance is conducting this illegal research entirely on its own
. Do not offload the blame to a rogue terrorist group. The horror comes from the legitimate government doing it.
There is no "Magic" fix. The Geth code cannot simply be talked down with a high persuasion roll. Oracle is operating on cold mathematical logic. The purge will happen unless physical intervention stops it.

The Irreducible Point
Project Perseus works because it exploits the inherent contradictions of the Mass Effect universe: organics rely completely on highly advanced technology for survival, yet are deeply terrified of that same technology achieving sentience
. The Alliance wants the power of a synthetic mind without granting it the rights of a living being
.
The protagonist (the Embedded Journalist) is the only person on board who operates outside the military chain of command. The soldiers are bound by orders; the scientists are blinded by curiosity; the AI is bound by survival logic. The protagonist has the privilege—and the cost—of deciding what the truth is worth.
