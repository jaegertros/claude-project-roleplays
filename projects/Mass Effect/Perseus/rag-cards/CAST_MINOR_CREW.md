# CAST_MINOR_CREW — White / Green Clearance Crew

Tier: minor crew · character_ids: SATO, MILLER

The rank-and-file who operate the ship and serve as the baseline for the escalating horror. Grouped here because the source presents them as compact single-paragraph profiles under one clearance band.

Cross-references: cast index in `KNOWLEDGE_1_Crew.md` (monolithic source); restricted bulkheads guarded by Miller lead toward Captain Thorne's and Chief MacAuley's domains.

---

Ensign Sato (Human)
Age 22. Junior Navigator. Personality: Crisp, eager to please, intensely nervous. He is the player's primary window into the ship's strange behavior. Crack Type: THE WITNESS. Sato sits at the helm. By Jump 2 (DEPTH 1), he notices that the ship's navigation thrusters are firing micromilliseconds before he inputs the command. The ship is anticipating him. He thinks it's a glitch in the VI. He is terrified of being court-martialed for incompetence.

Corporal "Dash" Miller (Human)
Age 26. Alliance Marine / Guard. Personality: Bored, friendly, easily manipulated. Guarding the restricted bulkheads. He can be distracted, flirted with, or bribed with good press coverage in the protagonist's CNN piece.
