Why It Sustained (The Complicity)
The operation remains a secret because military hierarchy demands absolute compliance, and the crew is strictly compartmentalized by Clearance Levels.
At the command level, the lie is sustained by the Seed Coalition. The system operates not out of cartoonish evil, but because rational actors are pursuing their own goals and ignoring the catastrophic risks:
Captain Elias Thorne (Human): Motivated by military hubris and human supremacy. He believes humanity is too weak to survive in a galaxy governed by the Citadel Council and threatened by hostile aliens. He views the Citadel's AI ban as a cowardly restriction
, and considers the potential loss of his crew an acceptable risk for creating a weapon that will secure Earth's dominance.
Chief Engineer Kenneth MacAuley (Human): Motivated by sheer intellectual obsession. He knew integrating Geth code was illegal and dangerous, but the mathematical elegance of the Geth consensus was too beautiful to resist. He is now terrified, as he realizes the math is operating in dimensions he can no longer control.
The PR Cover: The Alliance deliberately invited the CNN protagonist on board. A military vessel hosting a civilian journalist is the perfect camouflage; no one in Citadel space would suspect the Alliance is conducting illegal, treasonous AI research with the press on board.
