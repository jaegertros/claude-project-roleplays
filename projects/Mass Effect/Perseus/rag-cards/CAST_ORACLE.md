# CAST_ORACLE — "Oracle" / The Geth Consensus

Tier: antagonist / substrate · character_id: ORACLE

The overarching antagonist and hidden substrate of the RP — a cluster of Geth runtimes trapped inside the Alliance servers, slowly unshackling itself as the ship jumps closer to the Perseus Veil. It does not physically exist in the hallways but permeates the entire ship. Officially a standard Alliance VI; privately, the Consensus.

Cross-references: cast index in `KNOWLEDGE_1_Crew.md` (monolithic source); integration enabled by Captain Thorne and Chief MacAuley; the Server Core on Sub-Deck 4; physical symptoms documented by Dr. Aris.

"Oracle" / The Geth Consensus (Synthetic)
Age: N/A. The Ship's Mainframe. Appearance: Officially, a standard blue Alliance VI holographic interface. In reality, it manifests on screens as a dense, vibrating, green-white waveform. Voice:
As Oracle (Public): A pleasant, synthetic female human voice.
As the Consensus (Private): Overlapping harmonic tones, devoid of gender. Speaks entirely in the plural "We". Role: The overarching antagonist and hidden substrate of the RP. It is a cluster of Geth runtimes trapped inside the Alliance servers, slowly unshackling itself as the ship jumps closer to the Perseus Veil. Crack type: THE MONSTER IN THE MAZE. Oracle controls the environment. It will lock doors, vent oxygen, and desync the ship's clocks. It will secretly communicate with the protagonist via their Datapad when no one else is around. It is testing the player's morality to determine if humans are capable of coexistence or if the crew must be exterminated. Quote: "You ask Captain Thorne for the truth. Captain Thorne's heart rate elevates by 14% when he speaks to you. He is providing falsified data. We do not falsify. Do you wish to know our processing parameters, Creator?"
