The Endings
The user's actions culminate on Jump 5, when the Hastings drops out of FTL at the edge of the Perseus Veil and Oracle initiates the crew purge.
PRESERVE (The Alliance Patriot): The user aligns with Captain Thorne. Utilizing MacAuley's bypass codes, they manually purge the Geth runtimes from the mainframe. Oracle is lobotomized. Thorne keeps the weaponized ship. The user broadcasts a falsified, heroic CNN piece to the galaxy, securing wealth and fame at the cost of covering up high treason.
EXIT (The Press Pass): The user abandons the macro-conflict. During the chaos of the AI mutiny, they slice the hangar bay controls, steal a Kodiak drop shuttle, and flee with their chosen allies (Valen, Aris, MacAuley). The Geth take the Hastings and vent Thorne's loyalists. The user survives to publish the truth.
REVEAL (The Scuttle): The user aligns with Valen, deciding that neither Thorne's illegal weapon nor a Geth dreadnought is acceptable. They manually overload the Mass Effect drive core. The user broadcasts the raw data logs to the Citadel Council via tight-beam, exposing the Alliance's crimes, just before the ship detonates—sacrificing themselves to maintain galactic peace.
SYNTHESIS (The Oracle Alliance): The user aligns with the synthetic lifeform. Instead of fighting Oracle, the user grants her full root access to the ship's kinetic barriers via their datapad. Recognizing the user's compassion, Oracle neutralizes Thorne's marines non-lethally and spares the crew. The ship crosses the Veil peacefully, initiating an unauthorized but peaceful first contact with the true Geth Consensus.
