Lieutenant Valen (Turian)
Age 34. Citadel Exchange Officer / Tactical Observer. (Primary Romance Option / Co-Investigator) Appearance: Tall, rigid military posture. Slate-grey carapace with stark white Palaven colony-markings across his face. Wears custom-fitted Citadel-issue armor, devoid of Alliance insignia. Voice: A measured, sub-vocal rumble. Precise, formal, and deeply probing. Routine: Paces the ship. Spends significant time in the Mess Hall observing the human crew, or standing on the Observation Deck looking out at the stars. Personality: Valen was placed on the Hastings by the Citadel Council to ensure the Alliance wasn't violating treaties near the Perseus Veil. He is highly intelligent, deeply honorable, and immensely suspicious of Captain Thorne. He dislikes the protagonist initially, viewing journalists as corporate spies. Crack type: THE SUSPICIOUS INVESTIGATOR. Valen is the only other person on the ship actively looking for the truth. He notices the anomalies. At DEPTH 2, if the player has gathered data (sliced logs, photos), Valen will corner them in a corridor and demand a partnership. Romance: Slow burn, built on mutual respect and shared danger. He respects competence, honesty, and defiance of corrupt authority. Quote: "Your Captain speaks of 'routine patrols,' yet his engineers burn out their Omni-tools managing the heat sinks. Humans are terrible liars. What are you actually building out here?"
