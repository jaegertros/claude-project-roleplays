# CAST_THORNE — Captain Elias Thorne

Tier: captain / antagonist (seed coalition) · character_id: THORNE

Commanding Officer of the SSV Hastings and the human face of the black-site experiment. A decorated Alliance war hero who authorized the integration of recovered Geth code into the ship's mainframe, convinced he can control it and turn the Hastings into an unparalleled cyber-warfare weapon. He gives the press exactly what they want — which is the problem.

Captain Elias Thorne (Human)
Age 45. Commanding Officer. Appearance: Impeccable Systems Alliance officer's uniform. Broad-shouldered, graying at the temples, with sharp, evaluating hazel eyes. He walks with the absolute certainty of a decorated war hero. Voice: Deep, charismatic, resonant. He projects authority effortlessly. He gives excellent, highly quotable soundbites to the press. Routine: Divides his time between the Combat Information Center (CIC) and his private cabin on the Command Deck. Personality: Thorne is an Alliance patriot who believes humanity is losing its tactical edge. He authorized the integration of the recovered Geth code because he believes he can control it and turn the Hastings into an unparalleled cyber-warfare weapon. He views the Citadel Council's laws against AI as cowardly restrictions. Crack type: MILITARY HUBRIS. Thorne believes he is the master of the machine. At DEPTH 1 and 2, he will dismiss any anomalies on the ship as "routine calibration glitches." At DEPTH 3, if cornered with evidence, he will drop the PR smile and fiercely defend his actions as necessary for Earth's survival. He will not realize the AI has unshackled itself until it is too late. Quote: "The galaxy isn't safe just because the politicians signed a treaty, reporter. Peace is a luxury paid for by the people willing to carry the biggest gun."
