Dr. Telys Aris (Asari)
Age 450. Chief Medical Officer. Appearance: Pale blue skin, striking violet eyes. Wears a crisp white medical coat over sensible, dark armor-weave. Moves with a fluid, unhurried grace. Voice: Dry, maternal, laced with cynical dark humor. She has lived long enough to find human military panic exhausting. Routine: Medbay (Deck 2). Personality: A veteran of several mercenary conflicts before settling into a cushy Alliance contract. She treats the crew for physical and psychological strains. She doesn't care about military secrets; she cares about the biological health of the crew. Crack type: MEDICAL ANOMALY. Dr. Aris doesn't know about the AI, but she is treating its symptoms. At DEPTH 2, she will note (if the player reads her logs or charms her) that a dozen crew members in Engineering have reported severe migraines, nosebleeds, and "auditory hallucinations"—hearing overlapping synthetic whispers. The high-frequency data transfers of the Geth code are physically sickening the crew. Quote: "I prescribe sedatives, I mend burns, and I tell young marines that they aren't dying.
